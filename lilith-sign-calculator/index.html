Lilith calculator: Calculation of the Black Moon in zodiac signs by date of birth

Lilith, or Black Moon - this is not a physical celestial body, but an astronomical fictitious point. It represents the apogee of the lunar orbit - the point furthest away from the Earth, at which the Moon moves slowest.

In the natal chart, Lilith symbolizes the “shadow” side of the psyche: hidden desires, unconscious fears, karmic debts and temptations that a person will face throughout life. Understanding what zodiac sign your Black Moon is in helps you understand internal blocks and transform negative energy into a resource for spiritual growth.

How does Lilith calculation work?

The complete cycle of Lilith's revolution in the zodiac circle is approximately 8.85 years (about 3232 days). It stays in each sign for about 9 months. Our calculator uses a highly accurate mathematical model of “Mean Lilith” to determine its coordinates on the ecliptic.

The following formula is used for calculation:

$$P = ((T — E) / 86400000) \cdot V \pmod{360}$$

Where:

  • $P$ - final position in degrees (from 0° Aries);
  • $T$ - target time in milliseconds (Unix Timestamp);
  • $E$ - reference epoch (1704672000000 ms - Lilith entering 0° Aries on January 8, 2024);
  • $V$ - average angular speed ( $0.11136546^\circ$ per day).

Interpretation of results: three levels of elaboration

Astrologers distinguish three levels of how a person can experience the energy of Lilith. Our calculator will help you determine the starting point for analysis:

  1. First level (Active): A person derives pleasure from the manifestation of the “dark” qualities of the sign. He himself is a source of problems for those around him.

  3. Third level (Transformation): A person sees temptation in advance and consciously refuses it. Evil no longer has power over the individual.
