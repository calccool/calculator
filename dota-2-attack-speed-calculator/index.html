Dota 2 Attack Speed ​​Calculator: Calculate BAT, IAS and Hit Rate

Understanding the mechanics of attack speed is what separates the average player from the professional. In Dota 2, this parameter determines how often your hero hits, which directly affects the total damage per second (DPS) and the frequency of passive effects such as Bash or Chain Lightning.

This tool allows you to instantly calculate three critical indicators:

  • TAS (Total Attack Speed): total attack speed value.
  • APS (Attacks Per Second): the number of blows inflicted by the hero in one second.
  • Attack Interval: the exact time in seconds between two successive strikes.

Basic Variables and Formulas

The calculation is based on three key components. To independently calculate the parameters, the following mathematical models are used:

  1. TAS indicator: This is the sum of the base value (always 100), the hero's current agility, and any bonuses from items or abilities (IAS). $$TAS = 100 + Agility + IAS$$
  2. Attack Frequency (APS): Depends on the base attack time (BAT) of a particular hero. The lower the BAT, the more effectively each unit of attack speed scales. $$APS = \frac{TAS \times 0.01}{BAT}$$
  3. Attack cycle duration: This is the time that passes from the start of one swing to the opportunity to make the next one. $$Attack\ Duration = \frac{1}{APS}$$

Important game restrictions

In Dota 2, there are hard limits (caps) that must be taken into account when purchasing items such as Moon Shard:

  • Minimum threshold: 20 AS units.
  • Maximum threshold: 700 AS units.

Any attack speed value above 700 is not beneficial, so timing is critical to effectively distributing gold in the late game.

BAT reference table of popular heroes

Base Attack Time is an innate stat. Most heroes have a BAT of 1.7, but some characters have improved stats, making them better carries.

Effect of items on attack speed

You can use our calculator to figure out which item will give the biggest boost to your hit rate. Below is a list of popular artifacts and their contribution to IAS (Increased Attack Speed):

  • Moon Shard: +140 AS (the most powerful boost in one slot).
  • Mjollnir: +70 AS (+active acceleration).
  • Butterfly: +30 AS (plus agility bonus).
  • Hyperstone: +60 AS (base component for top artifacts).
  • Power Treads: +25 AS.

How to use the calculator

The interface is divided into logical blocks for maximum convenience. To get an accurate result, follow these steps:

Selecting basic parameters

You can select a specific hero from a preset list (for example, Juggernaut with his BAT 1.4) or switch to manual mode and enter a specific BAT value if you are under the influence of abilities that change this parameter (for example, Alchemist's ultimate).

Accounting for agility and bonuses

Enter your hero's current agility value from the game interface. The calculator will automatically equal 1 unit of agility to 1 unit of attack speed. In the "Extra Speed" field, add any bonuses from items such as Hyperstone or ally buffs (Bloodlust from Ogre Magi).

Visualization and analysis

After pressing the button "Calculate", the system will build a dynamic graph (Donut Chart) showing your proximity to the 700 AS limit, and will launch a sword animation, the rhythm of which completely coincides with the real attack speed of your hero in the game.
