Ozon commission calculator (FBO and FBS): accurate calculation of profit and unit economics

Selling on a marketplace requires pinpoint accuracy in calculations. An error of a few percentage points in the commission or incorrect accounting of product volume can turn a profitable niche into an unprofitable one. Our interactive calculator is designed specifically to allow sellers to instantly estimate their net profit before shipping goods to the warehouse.

Main calculation indicators

To get an accurate result, the calculator analyzes five key variables:

  • Sale price: The amount that a buyer pays on the site.
  • Cost price: Your costs for purchasing or producing a unit of goods.
  • Product category: Ozon commission percentage (varies from 5% to 19% and above).
  • Package volume: Affects the cost of the highway and processing.
  • Working model: Choose between FBO (sale from Ozon warehouse) and FBS (sale from your warehouse).

Mathematical model of Ozon expenses

The calculator algorithm is based on the official regulations of the marketplace. The final amount charged to the seller is calculated using the following formula:

$$Выплата = Цена — (Комиссия + Логистика + Последняя миля)$$

Where the main components are calculated like this:

1. Sales commission

This is a fixed percentage of the selling price, depending on the category.

$$K = P \cdot \frac{percent}{100}$$

2. Logistics (Highway)

The cost of transportation depends on the volumetric weight. For standard shipments up to 1 litre, the base rate applies and a surcharge applies for each additional litre. The FBS model also adds processing costs at the receiving point.

3. Last mile

Payment for delivery of goods to a specific point of delivery or by courier to the buyer. Makes up 5.5% from the price of the product, but has strict limits:

  • Minimum: 20 $
  • Maximum: 500 $

How to use the calculator

  1. Select mode: Switch between FBO and FBS at the top of the form to compare the benefits of different schemes.
  2. Enter financial details: Indicate the planned price on the display and the actual cost.
  3. Specify product parameters: Select a category (% of commission depends on this) and volume in liters. For convenience, we have added quick selection buttons (1L, 3L, 5L).
  4. Click "Calculate": The system will instantly generate a detailed receipt with all deductions and build a diagram of the cost structure.

Why is it important to consider ROI and margin?

Our calculator not only shows the remaining money, but also calculates ROI (Return on Investment). This is an indicator of the effectiveness of your investment:

$$ROI = \frac{Чистая прибыль}{Себестоимость} \cdot 100\%$$

If your ROI is below 20%, any promotion or forced discount from the marketplace can take you into the red. Use the visualization block in the calculator to clearly see what share of your revenue is consumed by logistics, and what share is real earnings.
