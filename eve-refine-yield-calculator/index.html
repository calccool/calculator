EVE Online Ore Refining Calculator (Refine Yield)

The efficiency of mining in EVE Online depends not only on the volume of ore excavated, but also on how well you can process (refine) it. Our calculator takes into account all the variables - from basic structural characteristics to advanced processing skills - to give you the most accurate forecast of the amount of minerals you will produce.

Refine Yield calculation formula

The game uses a multiplicative calculation model. The final percentage of mineral yield is determined by the following mathematical formula:

$$Yield = Base \times (1 + Repro \times 0.03) \times (1 + ReproEff \times 0.02) \times (1 + OreSkill \times 0.02) \times (1 + Implant)$$

Where:

  • Base — base indicator of a station or citadel;
  • Repro — Reprocessing skill level;
  • ReproEff — Reprocessing Efficiency skill level;
  • OreSkill — level of specialized skill for a specific type of ore (for example, Veldspar Processing);
  • Implant — bonus from the “Beancounter” series implant installed in the head (RX-801, 802 or 804).

Features of processing: batches and cycles

It is important to remember that in EVE Online, ore processing occurs in strictly fixed batches. For most base ores (Veldspar, Scordite, Plagioclase, Omber), the batch size is 100 units.

If the amount of ore in your warehouse is not a multiple of 100, the remainder after dividing by the batch size will not be processed and will remain in the hangar. Our calculator automatically calculates the number of "Full Cycles", discarding incomplete batches so you can see the actual amount of minerals that will end up in your inventory.

Composition of minerals in base ore

Each ore type contains a unique set of minerals. Using our tool, you can instantly estimate the volume of the following minerals:

  1. Tritanium - the basis of any production, is found in abundance in Veldspar.
  2. Pyerite - An important component for armor, found in Scordite and Plagioclase.
  3. Mexallon - required for advanced modules, obtained from Plagioclase.
  4. Isogen - a valuable mineral characteristic of Omber ore.

How to use the calculator

  1. Select ore type: Indicate the specific type of raw material you plan to process.
  2. Enter quantity: Enter the total number of ore units in your hold or warehouse.
  3. Set station conditions: Choose a base percentage (from 50% at NPC stations to T2 rig bonuses at zeros).
  4. Set up skills and implants: Turn on the "Character Skills" block if you are a professional miner to take into account your leveling.
  5. Click "Calculate": The system will instantly build an efficiency chart and display a final list of minerals.

Use this data to plan logistics and estimate the market value of your cargo before selling or manufacturing.
