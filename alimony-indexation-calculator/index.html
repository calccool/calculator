Calculator for indexation of alimony in a fixed amount (TDS)

Alimony established by the court in fixed cash amount (TDS), are not static. To prevent inflation from eating up child benefits, Russian legislation provides for their automatic indexation. The main guideline here is living wage (LM) for the corresponding socio-demographic group (usually “children”).

Our calculator allows you to instantly calculate changes in payments using current data from regional or federal PM values.

Basic functions of the calculator

  • Indexation of the amount: calculation of the current payment amount when the PM changes.
  • Debt calculation: determination of the total amount of debt for a specific period (in months) taking into account the indexed amount.
  • Growth visualization: visual graphs and diagrams of the percentage increase in payments.
  • Downgrade protection: automatic control of the rule “alimony does not decrease”, even if the new monthly minimum amount is lower than the previous one.

Mathematical formula for calculation

Indexation is based on the principle of maintaining the “multiplicity” of alimony to the subsistence minimum, which was established at the time of the court decision.

Mathematically this is expressed by the formula:

$$S_{new} = \left( \frac{S_{base}}{PM_{old}} \right) \times PM_{new}$$

Where:

  • $S_{new}$ - new amount of alimony after indexation;
  • $S_{base}$ - basic amount of alimony by court decision;
  • $PM_{old}$ - the cost of living at the time of appointment or previous indexation;
  • $PM_{new}$ - current (new) cost of living.

Important legal nuances

When using the calculator and planning collection, it is important to remember several rules enshrined in the Family Code and decisions of the Plenum of the Supreme Court:

1. Who should index?

Indexation must be carried out by:

  • The payer's employer (if the amount of alimony does not exceed 100 thousand rubles).
  • Bailiff in enforcement proceedings.
  • The payer himself (voluntarily), so that the debt does not accumulate.

2. If the cost of living has decreased

This is a rare but possible situation. According to the law, if the cost of living has decreased, the amount of alimony remains the same. Our calculator takes this limitation into account automatically: you will see a notification that the amount is fixed.

3. Regional or federal PM?

First of all, the PM established in the subject of the Russian Federation at the place of residence of the alimony recipient is used. If such an indicator is not established in the region, the calculation is based on the all-Russian subsistence minimum.

4. Retrospective calculation (debt)

If indexation has not been carried out for several years, the recipient has the right to demand recalculation for the entire period. In this case, the calculator will help calculate the debt based on the “layering” of changes in the monthly minimum for each year.
