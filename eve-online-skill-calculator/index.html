Skill leveling time calculator in EVE Online

The development system in EVE Online is unique: skills are learned passively, even when you are not in the game. However, the speed of this process is variable. It directly depends on your characteristics (Attributes) and the multiplier of a specific skill (Rank).

Basic formula for calculating SP

Each skill is tied to two attributes: primary and secondary. The rate at which Skill Points accumulate is calculated using the following formula:

$$SP_{min} = Primary + \frac{Secondary}{2}$$

If your character is in Alpha Clone status, the final result is divided by 2. Omega status allows you to use the full power of your brain, providing a coefficient of $1.0$ .

Table of basic SP costs by level

Regardless of the difficulty of the skill, the proportions of points required to achieve levels 1 to 5 are always the same. The total amount of SP for each level is calculated as the product of the base value and the skill's Rank multiplier.

Key factors affecting pumping speed

To outpace competitors and use their time effectively, a capsuleer needs to consider three critical aspects:

  • Remapping: You can change your attribute values ​​once per year (plus bonus attempts). Focusing on the right parameters (for example, Intelligence and Memory for engineering skills) can reduce training time by weeks.
  • Use of implants: Installing neural boosters in slots 1 to 5 adds +1 to +5 to the corresponding attributes, which directly increases $SP_{min}$ .
  • Clone status: Omega status doesn't just double your speed, it gives you access to advanced skills and allows for an endless learning queue.

Skill multiplier (Rank)

The difficulty of skills in EVE ranges from simple (Rank 1, for example, Navigation ) to extremely difficult (Rank 16, for example, Titan ). The higher the rank, the more time it takes to complete each level. For example, to learn a rank 5 skill to level 5 you will need:

$$256,000 \times 5 = 1,280,000 \text{ SP}$$

Tips for optimizing your Skill Plan

  1. Group skills: Try to learn skills with the same basic attributes back to back to maximize the benefit of a single remap.
  2. Use "Biology": Leveling up your skill Biology increases the duration of temporary accelerators (Cerebral Accelerators), which provide a powerful boost to all characteristics.
  3. Do not neglect implants of the “Standard” or “Improved” series: Even if you lose a ship in PvP, your implants in the capsule remain with you until the capsule itself is destroyed. This is a long-term investment in your character's capital.
