Warframe Dojo Research Cost Calculator

Building and researching in the Dojo is one of the most resource-intensive mechanics in Warframe. The main difficulty lies in dynamic scaling: the base cost indicated in Wikipedia or game databases is relevant only for Phantom Clan. As soon as a clan increases its member limit (tier level), the cost of all active and future projects increases exponentially.

Our calculator allows you to instantly calculate the final price of any blueprint, be it weapons, Warframe components or Dojo rooms, saving you from manual calculations and errors when planning your clan budget.

Table of cost multipliers depending on the clan's tier

There are 5 main clan levels in Warframe. Each level increases the cost of resources by a fixed factor.

Mathematical calculation model

For accurate planning, we use two basic formulas. The first determines the total cost of the project, the second determines the individual load on each active player, taking into account existing reserves in the treasury (Dojo Storage).

Total cost formula:

$$C_{total} = C_{base} \times M$$

Where:

  • $C_{total}$ - total cost of resources;
  • $C_{base}$ - base cost for the Phantom Clan;
  • $M$ - clan tier multiplier.

Individual contribution formula:

$$C_{per\_member} = \frac{C_{total} — V}{N_{active}}$$

Where:

  • $V$ - the number of resources already added to the storage;
  • $N_{active}$ - the number of participants ready to chip in.

How to use the calculator

To get an accurate result, follow these instructions:

  1. Please indicate the base price: Enter the amount of resources required for the Phantom Clan. We have added a quick input button for Hema, since its research is the most difficult test for any clan leader.
  2. Select clan tier: Indicate the current status of your clan (from Ghost to Lunar).
  3. Use advanced mode: If some of the resources have already been contributed or you want to divide the expenses between friends, activate the “Calculate participant contributions” switch.
  4. Analyze the visualization: Our calculator builds a pie chart that clearly shows the share of the base in the total cost or the percentage of research readiness when taking into account contributions.

Tips for managing resources in the Dojo

  • Plan ahead: If you plan to increase your clan's tier, complete all expensive research (such as Fieldron Injector, Mutagenic Mass) at the minimum level.
  • Use Storage: Resources obtained from demolishing old rooms or decorations go into the Storage. Always check its condition before requesting contributions from players.
  • Fairness of contributions: In large clans, not all players are active. Use the Active Members field to ensure that the per person calculation is realistic and not formal.
