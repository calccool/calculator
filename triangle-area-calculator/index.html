Triangle Calculator: Calculate Area, Perimeter and Sides (Online)

Online solution of triangles of any complexity

The triangle is a basic figure in geometry, but calculating its parameters is often difficult due to the abundance of formulas. Our universal calculator replaces a reference book on geometry. It not only gives an answer, but also determines the type of figure, checks the possibility of its existence and calculates additional parameters (heights, angles).

The tool supports 4 main calculation methods, covering 99% of problems.

1. Heron's formula (on three sides)

The most popular method is when the lengths of all sides are known ( $a, b, c$ ), but the height is unknown.

First the semi-perimeter ( $p$ ) is calculated:

$$p = \frac{a + b + c}{2}$$

Then the area ( $S$ ) is found using the formula:

$$S = \sqrt{p(p — a)(p — b)(p — c)}$$

Important: The calculator automatically checks the "triangle inequality" ( $a + b > c$ ). If it is not possible to construct a shape with the given sides, you will see an error message.

2. Classic method (through base and height)

Basic school formula. Used if the length of one side (base $a$ ) and the height ( $h$ ) dropped onto it are known.

$$S = \frac{1}{2} \cdot a \cdot h$$

3. Trigonometric method (through angle)

If two sides ( $a$ and $b$ ) and the angle between them ( $\gamma$ ) are known, the area is calculated using the sine:

$$S = \frac{1}{2} \cdot a \cdot b \cdot \sin(\gamma)$$

4. Equilateral triangle

A special case when all sides are equal ( $a$ ). The formula is simplified as much as possible:

$$S = \frac{\sqrt{3}}{4} \cdot a^2$$
