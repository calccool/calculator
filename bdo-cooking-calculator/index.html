In order not to go into the red when purchasing ingredients at an auction, you need to take into account the skill of the craft, current prices for resources and imperial delivery bonuses.

Imperial Delivery: The Gold Standard of Income

Imperial delivery (cook packs) is the foundation of your well-being. Unlike an auction, NPCs purchase goods at a fixed price that increases based on your skill level.

Main variables in the calculation:

  • Influence Points (IP): Determines your daily change limit.
  • Mastery: Gives a percentage increase to the base cost of the pack.
  • Chest Rank: From “Student” to “Specialist”. The higher the rank, the higher the base price.

Formula for calculating imperial profits

To determine the total revenue, the following mathematical model is used:

$$TotalProfit = (BasePrice \times 2.5 + BasePrice \times \frac{MasteryBonus}{100}) \times \frac{CP}{2} — (CostPerBox \times \frac{CP}{2})$$

Where:

  • $BasePrice$ - base price of the chest;
  • $MasteryBonus$ - your skill bonus as a percentage;
  • $CP$ - influence points;
  • $CostPerBox$ - the cost of crafting one chest.

Trading through auction: Risks and taxes

Selling prepared meals or ingredients at the central market can earn more silver than an imperial, but this is where the BDO tax system.

Without using the Premium Set, you lose 35% of the transaction amount. With an active set, the effective commission is reduced and you receive 84.5% of the lot price.

What is important to consider:

  • Current price: Auction prices are dynamic and depend on demand.
  • Cost price: Includes the cost of the stove, the purchase of resources and time.
  • Volume: Selling 100,000 units of vinegar is more difficult and takes longer than selling 200 packs to NPCs.

Tips for maximizing profits

  1. Keep RH at maximum: The daily limit is half of your influence points. Every 2 OB is an extra pack per day.
  2. Accelerate your skills: Use Manos clothes, jewelry and buffs (seafood croissant, green elixir) to increase the percentage markup.
  3. Count the by-products: When crafting, you receive a “Witch's Delicacy”, which can be exchanged for additional CP or milk, which indirectly increases your income.
