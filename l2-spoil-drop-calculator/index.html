Main factors influencing the chance

The math behind L2 drops is more complex than simply multiplying the base number by the rates. The final result is influenced by:

  • Server Rates: Multiplier set by the administration (x1, x3, x10, etc.).
  • Premium Account (PA): Individual character bonus, which is usually multiplied with server rates.
  • Level Penalty: If your character's level is 3 or more higher than the monster's level, a dynamic chance reduction takes effect.
  • Mechanics of Deep Blue: With a difference of 8-9 levels, the chance is practically zero, which makes farming in low-level locations pointless.

Formula for calculating the final chance

The calculation uses a combined formula that takes into account all active modifiers:

$$P_{final} = P_{base} \times Rate_{server} \times (1 + \frac{Bonus_{PA}}{100}) \times Penalty_{lvl}$$

Where:

  • $P_{base}$ - base chance of an item from the database (x1).
  • $Penalty_{lvl}$ - penalty coefficient (from 1.0 to 0.01).

Chance of success per killstreak

Many players make the mistake of thinking that with a 1% chance they are guaranteed to get an item for 100 kills. In reality it works differently. To calculate the probability of receiving at least one subject for $N$ attempts, the binomial distribution formula is used:

$$P_{total} = 1 — (1 — p)^n$$

Where:

  • $p$ - final decimal chance (for example, 0.05 for 5%).
  • $n$ - number of monsters killed.

Practical advice for farming

  1. Keep track of the level: If you swing too fast, your spoiler will start to fade. It is optimal to keep the difference no more than 2 levels for maximum profit.
  2. Overdraw effect (100%+): If the final chance exceeds 100%, our calculator will show the expected number of items from one mob. For example, with a 250% chance you are guaranteed to get 2 units of a resource, and with a 50% chance you will get a third.
  3. Guarantor of production: We have added the field "Guarantor (99%)". This is the number of mobs that must be destroyed in order to obtain the coveted item with a mathematical probability of 99%. This number helps you plan the duration of your farming session.

Drop mechanics FAQ

  • Does the mob's level affect the chance if I'm younger than it? No, the penalty only works towards the player who is older than the mob. If you beat red mobs, the chance corresponds to the rates.
  • Do PA and runes stack? On most servers, PA and event rune bonuses add up to the total bonus percentage ( $Bonus_{PA}$ ).
  • Why does the calculator show 1% when I beat a level 85 mob of level 20? This is an economy protection mechanic that prevents high-level characters from farming valuable resources in the starting locations.
