Vacation days calculator in Kazakhstan including holidays

Planning a vacation in the Republic of Kazakhstan requires careful consideration of public holidays. Unlike many other countries, in the Republic of Kazakhstan, holidays that fall during the period of paid annual leave are not included in its duration. This means that your holiday is actually extended by the number of holiday days.

Our smart calculator is designed specifically to automate this process. It doesn’t just add days, but analyzes the calendar of holidays in the Republic of Kazakhstan and determines the exact date of your return to the workplace.

Legislative basis for calculation

According to Article 90 of the Labor Code of the Republic of Kazakhstan, the duration of paid annual leave is calculated in calendar days without taking into account public holidays, which are days off.

Important nuances of legislation:

  • Paid leave is measured in calendar days.
  • State and national holidays extend leave.
  • The religious holidays Eid al-Adha and Christmas are days off, but not public holidays, so they included in the total duration of vacation as usual days.

Formula for calculating the end date of vacation

Mathematically, the calculation of the $D_{work}$ start date can be represented as follows:

$$D_{end} = D_{start} + (N_{vac} + N_{hol}) — 1$$

Where:

  • $D_{start}$ — date of the first day of vacation;
  • $N_{vac}$ — number of requested calendar days of vacation;
  • $N_{hol}$ - the number of public holidays that fell during this period;
  • $D_{end}$ - the last day of actual rest.

After determining $D_{end}$ , the calculator finds the nearest working day $D_{work}$ , skipping weekends according to your 5 or 6 day work week schedule.
