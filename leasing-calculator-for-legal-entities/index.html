Leasing calculator for legal entities: online calculation of payments and tax benefits

Leasing is an effective financial instrument that allows legal entities and individual entrepreneurs to renew fixed assets (cars, special equipment, equipment) without withdrawing large sums from circulation. Our calculator is designed specifically for preliminary calculation of a leasing transaction, taking into account the specifics of taxation in the Russian Federation.

Basic calculation parameters

To get an accurate result, you must fill in the following fields:

  • Property value: full price of the leased item including VAT.
  • Advance payment: down payment (usually ranges from 10% to 50%). The higher the advance, the lower the monthly payment and the final increase in price.
  • Leasing term: period of validity of the contract in months.
  • Annual price increase: the average percentage increase in property value per year. It is important to understand that this is a marketing metric and is different from the effective interest rate.

Tax advantages on OSNO

One of the main advantages of leasing for companies on the general taxation system is the possibility of significantly reducing the tax burden. The calculator automatically calculates:

  1. VAT refund (20%): the entire amount of leasing payments includes VAT, which is deductible.
  2. Savings on income tax (20%): leasing payments are expensed, which reduces the tax base.
  3. Accelerated depreciation: allows you to write off the value of property 3 times faster than when buying on credit.

Formulas for calculating leasing payments

The mathematical logic of the calculator is based on calculating the annuity schedule taking into account the increase in price.

Total amount of the contract

The amount of all obligations of the lessee is calculated using the formula:

$$S_{total} = P + (P \cdot \frac{K}{100} \cdot \frac{T}{12})$$

Where:

  • $S_{total}$ — total contract amount;
  • $P$ - property value;
  • $K$ - percentage of annual price increase;
  • $T$ — leasing term in months.

Monthly payment

Amount of regular payment after making an advance:

$$M = \frac{S_{total} — A}{T}$$

Where:

  • $M$ - monthly payment;
  • $A$ — advance amount in rubles.

Advantages of leasing for business development

The use of leasing allows the company to maintain liquidity and effectively manage the budget. Key benefits include:

  • Minimum withdrawal: It is enough to make a small advance to start using the equipment.
  • No additional collateral: The subject of security is the property itself.
  • Flexible schedules: the ability to customize payments according to the seasonality of the business (decreasing, equal or seasonal schedules).
  • Easy approval: leasing companies are more loyal than banks, since the risk is protected by ownership of the object.
