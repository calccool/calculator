What is the sum of two cubes?

Sum of two cubes is an algebraic expression of the form $a^3 + b^3$ . The main value of this formula lies in its expansion, which helps you simplify expressions, reduce fractions, and solve equations.

The decomposition formula looks like this:

$$a^3 + b^3 = (a + b)(a^2 — ab + b^2)$$

Where:

  • $(a+b)$ is sum of numbers.
  • $(a^2 — ab + b^2)$ — partial squared difference these numbers.

Example calculation

Consider the classic example of $a = 2$ and $b = 3$ .

Step 1: Calculate cubes of numbers

  • $a^3 = 2^3 = 2 \cdot 2 \cdot 2 = **8**$
  • $b^3 = 3^3 = 3 \cdot 3 \cdot 3 = **27**$

Step 2: Find the sum of cubes

  • $a^3 + b^3 = 8 + 27 = **35**$

Step 3: Check using the decomposition formula

  • Sum of numbers: $(a+b) = 2 + 3 = **5**$
  • Partial squared difference: $(a^2 — ab + b^2) = (2^2 — (2 \cdot 3) + 3^2) = (4 — 6 + 9) = **7**$
  • Product of multipliers: $5 \cdot 7 = **35**$

The results coincide, which confirms the correctness of the formula.

Why is this formula important in mathematics?

Using the sum of two cubes formula has several key advantages:

  • Simplifying expressions: Allows you to replace a complex term in a cube with a product of simpler binomials.
  • Solving equations: Factoring often helps to find the roots of cubic equations.
  • Foundation for Higher Mathematics: The principles of FSU are actively used in integral calculus, as well as when working with complex numbers.
