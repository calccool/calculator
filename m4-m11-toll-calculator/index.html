Cost calculator for toll roads M-4 “Don” and M-11 “Neva”

Planning a trip along the federal highways M-4 Don (Moscow - Voronezh - Rostov-on-Don - Krasnodar) and M-11 Neva (Moscow - St. Petersburg) requires taking into account many variables. Our interactive tool allows you to instantly get detailed cost estimates, visualized in an easy-to-use chart.

Instructions for use

  1. Select route: Switch between tabs M-4 and M-11 to activate the corresponding basic tariffs.
  2. Specify distance: Enter the length of toll sections manually or use the quick entry buttons for popular destinations (for example, to Voronezh or St. Petersburg).
  3. Select vehicle class: From a regular motorcycle to a heavy truck, class critically affects the final multiplier.
  4. Specify additional conditions: Please check if your trip is on a weekend or you have a T-pass transponder to receive a discount.

Factors influencing the fare price

The fare is not a fixed amount. It is formed based on the base rate per kilometer, which is multiplied by coefficients depending on the type of transport you have and the travel time.

Mathematical calculation model

The algorithm of our calculator is based on the following formula:

$$S_{total} = (D \cdot R_{base} \cdot K_{class} \cdot K_{time}) \cdot (1 — D_{trans})$$

Where:

  • $S_{total}$ — final fare;
  • $D$ — distance of toll sections in kilometers;
  • $R_{base}$ — base rate for 1 km (for M-4 $\approx 4.2$ rub, for M-11 $\approx 5.5$ rub);
  • $K_{class}$ — vehicle class coefficient;
  • $K_{time}$ — weekend coefficient (is $1.2$ );
  • $D_{trans}$ - discount when using a transponder ( $0.15$ or $15\%$ ).

Advantages of using a T-pass transponder

Using a contactless payment method is not only convenient (passing through gateways without stopping), but also significant budget savings.

  • Basic discount: At most sites, transponder owners receive an automatic 15% discount.
  • Loyalty program: Accumulating points allows you to activate additional discounts of up to 15% for the next month.
  • No queues: Special dedicated lanes allow you to bypass congestion at toll booths.
  • Transparency: All write-offs are displayed in the mobile application in real time.

Features of the M-4 and M-11 highways

Each highway has its own pricing specifics:

  • M-4 "Don": The cost of travel in the summer season (from May to September) is often higher due to the increased tourist flow to the Black Sea.
  • M-11 "Neva": The price depends greatly on the site. For example, the section of the Golden Ring (15-58 km) is managed by another operator (SZKK), and the tariffs there are significantly higher than the average for the route.

Useful tips for drivers

  • Check your transponder balance at least 30-60 minutes before entering the toll section, as money may be credited with a delay.
  • At night (from 00:00 to 07:00) reduced tariffs apply in some areas, which allows you to save another 20-40% of the cost.
  • If you are traveling with a high roof rack, the system may mistakenly classify you as 2nd class instead of 1st. In this case, use the contact the operator button.
