Medical card expiration calculator

This professional tool is intended for workers in the food industry, medicine, education and service industries. It allows you to instantly determine the date of the next scheduled inspection and certification, based on the legislative norms of the Russian Federation.

How to use an online calculator

To get an accurate result, you do not need to independently study complex tables from the Order of the Ministry of Health. All you need to do is follow three simple steps:

  • Select mode: “General medical examination” for annual examinations or “Sanminimum” for monitoring the timing of hygienic training.
  • Please enter the date: Select the day on the calendar when you last had a medical examination.
  • Set frequency: Choose a period (6, 12 or 24 months) depending on your specific work.

Our algorithm uses the built-in visualization system “Validity Scale”. It clearly shows what percentage of time is left until the critical date, coloring the result green, yellow or red depending on the degree of risk of delay.

Mathematical calculation model

The calculation uses the formula of adding calendar intervals to the base date of the inspection.

The final date ( $D_{exp}$ ) and remaining days ( $D_{rem}$ ) are calculated using the following formulas:

$$D_{exp} = D_{last} + M_{period}$$

$$D_{rem} = \lceil \frac{D_{exp} — D_{now}}{86400000} \rceil$$

Where:

  • $D_{last}$ - date of last medical examination;
  • $M_{period}$ — validity period in months (according to the specifics of the work);
  • $D_{now}$ - current date;
  • $86400000$ - the number of milliseconds in one day.

Why is it important to schedule your appointment in advance?

One of the unique features of our calculator is "Pre-registration". When you activate this switch, the system automatically calculates the date 14 days before the document expires.

This is critical because:

  1. It takes time for doctors to pass through: On average, a full commission lasts from 2 to 5 business days.
  2. Waiting for test results: Some bacteriological studies take up to 7-10 days to prepare.
  3. Legal security: The employer is obliged to suspend the employee from work the next day after the expiration of the medical book without pay.
