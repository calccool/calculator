Calculation methodology and formulas

To calculate individual fate codes, the method of adding numbers is used to obtain a number not exceeding 22. If the final value is greater than 22, 22 is subtracted from it (the method of numerological reduction according to the Major Arcana system).

The mathematical model for calculating the Triad is as follows:

  1. Arcana of Personality ( $A_1$ ): Birthday energy. This is a person’s “calling card”, the way society perceives him. $$A_1 = \begin{cases} D, & \text{если } D \le 22 \\ D — 22, & \text{если } D > 22 \end{cases}$$ Where $D$ is the birthday number.
  2. Arcana of Lessons ( $A_2$ ): Energy of the month of birth. Responsible for the spiritual school and tasks that a person needs to solve in the first half of life. $$A_2 = M$$ Where $M$ is the serial number of the month (from 1 to 12).
  3. Arcana of Destination ( $A_3$ ): The sum of all digits of the date of birth, reduced to the range 1–22. This is the main vector of development and the final mission. $$S = \sum \text{цифр даты (гггг + мм + дд)}$$ $$A_3 = \text{сокращение } S \text{ до диапазона [1, 22]}$$

Why is it important to know your Arcana of Fate?

How to transfer energy from “minus” to “plus”

If you feel stagnant in business or emotional burnout, most likely your main Arcana has gone into “minus” (shadow manifestation).

  • For Arcana of Power (for example, 4 Emperor): it is important to abandon despotism and control in favor of wise mentoring.
  • For Arcana of creativity (for example, 17 Star): it is necessary to overcome “star fever” or, conversely, excessive uncertainty and begin to show your talent to the world.
  • For Arcana of knowledge (for example, 5 Hierophant): You should stop lecturing others and become an eternal learner, open to new information.
