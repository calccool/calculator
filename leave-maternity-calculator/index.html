Legislative framework and calculation rules

According to Article 256 of the Labor Code of the Russian Federation, parental leave can last until the child reaches three years of age. However, financial payments (benefits) are usually limited to one and a half years.

Date calculation algorithm

The calculation is based on two key rules:

  1. Last day of vacation - this is the child’s birthday (when he turns exactly 1.5 years old or 3 years old). On this day, the employee still retains the right to rest and benefits.
  2. First working day - the next calendar day after your birthday.

Mathematically it looks like this:

$$D_{work} = D_{birth} + P_{leave} + 1 \text{ день}$$

Where:

  • $D_{work}$ - date of return to work;
  • $D_{birth}$ - date of birth of the child;
  • $P_{leave}$ - vacation period (1.5 or 3 years).

Important clarification: If the calculated date of $D_{work}$ falls on an official holiday according to the 5/2 schedule, then the actual day of release is considered to be the first working day following it.
