Calculator for calculating night hours with a shift schedule online: calculation according to the Labor Code of the Russian Federation

Working at night is not only physical activity, but also a special payment procedure regulated by labor legislation. Our calculator is designed specifically to accurately divide working time into day and night segments, which is critical for the correct calculation of wages during a shift work schedule.

According to Article 96 of the Labor Code of the Russian Federation, night time is considered to be the period from 22:00 to 06:00. Any work during this interval must be paid at an increased rate compared to work under normal conditions.

How to use the calculator

To get instant results, just follow three simple steps:

  1. Specify time: Select the start and end times of your shift. The calculator automatically recognizes midnight crossings (for example, from 20:00 to 08:00).
  2. Enter a break: Enter the total duration of rest in minutes. The algorithm will proportionally distribute this time between day and night periods.
  3. Set up payment: Turn on the “Calculate earnings” toggle switch, indicate your hourly rate and the amount of the bonus.

Key rules and calculation formulas

The mathematical model of the calculator is based on the summarized accounting of working hours. If your shift overlaps with the interval 22:00 – 06:00, payment is calculated according to the following methodology:

1. Calculation of the total duration of the shift:

If the end time is less than the start time, the shift is considered to end on the next day:

$$T_{shift} = (T_{end} + 24:00) — T_{start}$$

2. Night time definition:

Each hour of operation is checked to see if it belongs to the interval $[22:00, 06:00]$ .

3. Proportional break deduction:

To make the calculation fair, rest time is deducted from night and day hours in proportion to their share in the total shift:

$$H_{night\_clean} = H_{night\_total} — (Break \cdot \frac{H_{night\_total}}{T_{shift}})$$

4. Financial result:

The total amount per shift is calculated using the formula:

$$S = (T_{total} \cdot Rate) + (H_{night\_clean} \cdot Rate \cdot \frac{Bonus\%}{100})$$

where $Rate$ is the hourly tariff rate, and $Bonus\%$ is the surcharge percentage for night hours.

Legislative framework

It is important to remember that according to Decree of the Government of the Russian Federation No. 554, the minimum increase in wages for work at night is 20% hourly tariff rate for each hour of work. However, in a collective agreement or local regulatory act of an enterprise, this percentage may be higher.

Our calculator allows you to flexibly adjust the percentage of additional payment so that you can adapt the calculation to the conditions of your employment contract.
