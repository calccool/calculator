How does the Dukan calculator work?

Our algorithm takes into account not only standard parameters (height and current weight), but also critical individual characteristics:

  • Gender and Age: Metabolism changes over the years, and what comes easily in your 20s takes longer in your 40s.
  • Body Type (Bone): We have added wrist width considerations. People with wide bones (hypersthenics) physiologically cannot weigh as much as asthenics of the same height.
  • Weight history: The number of previous diets and pregnancies influences the rate of metabolic response.

Ideal weight formula

The calculation is based on the modified Lorentz formula, adapted by Dr. Dukan. The basic equations look like this:

For men:

$IdealWeight = (Height — 100) — \frac{Height — 150}{4}$

For women:

$IdealWeight = (Height — 100) — \frac{Height — 150}{2.5}$

Where Height is height in centimeters.

Coefficients that take into account age (+0.1 kg for each year over 20 years) and the type of bone structure are applied to the resulting base value.

4 Stages of the Dukan Diet: Your Roadmap

The calculator automatically generates the duration of each phase. Here's what they mean and what rules apply at each stage.

1. Attack (Attack Phase)

The shortest and most aggressive stage. Its purpose is to trigger the process of ketosis and provide quick weight loss for motivation.

  • Duration: Usually from 2 to 7 days (calculated using a calculator depending on excess weight).
  • Diet: 72 permitted protein products (meat, fish, poultry, low-fat dairy products).
  • Necessarily: 1.5 tbsp. spoons of oat bran per day, 1.5 liters of water, 20 minutes of walking.

2. Alternation or Cruise (Cruise Phase)

The main stage in which you will gradually lose weight until you reach the “Correct Weight”.

  • Duration: Calculated based on a loss rate of ~1 kg per week.
  • Mode: Alternating days of pure protein (PP) and protein-vegetable days (VP). Schemes: 1/1 (recommended), 5/5.
  • Diet: 28 types of vegetables are added to the proteins.
  • Necessarily: 2 tbsp. spoons of oat bran, 30 minutes walk.

3. Consolidation Phase

The most vulnerable period. The weight has been reached, but the body wants to gain it back. The purpose of the stage is to prevent the “yo-yo effect”.

  • Duration: The formula is strict - 10 days for every kilogram lost. For example, if you lost 10 kg, the stage will last 100 days.
  • Diet: Gradual return of starchy foods, cheese, whole grain bread. The concept of a “Festive meal” is introduced (2 times a week).
  • Thursday Rule: One day a week (classically - Thursday) should be purely protein, as on Attack.
  • Necessarily: 2.5 tbsp. spoons of bran.

4. Stabilization Phase

This is not a diet, but a lifestyle. There are no restrictions on products, but there are three iron rules that must be followed for life so that the weight does not return.
