Gua number calculator online: calculation by date of birth and Feng Shui directions

The Gua number is a person’s personal energy code in the Feng Shui system (Ba Zhai direction), which is calculated based on the year of birth and gender. This is a “digital key” to the Earth’s magnetic field, determining which cardinal directions bring you vitality and which deplete it.

Knowing your number allows you to correctly orient your bed, desk, and even your front door to resonate with the favorable flows of Chi energy. Our online calculator automatically takes into account the Chinese solar calendar and substitution rules for the "central" number 5, providing you with an accurate sector map.

How to use the Gua number calculator

To get accurate results, follow three simple steps:

  1. Select gender: Energy formulas for men and women are fundamentally different.
  2. Enter year of birth: Enter the full four-digit year according to the Gregorian calendar.
  3. Please note your date of birth: This is a critical point. In Feng Shui, the New Year does not begin on January 1, but at the beginning of February (the “Li Chun” period). If you were born between January 1 and February 4 inclusive, be sure to activate the corresponding switch in the calculator - the calculation will be made based on the previous year.

Calculation algorithm: Mathematics Feng Shui

The calculator is based on the classic Lo-shu algorithm. First, the base number $S$ is calculated as the remainder of the year of birth divided by 9.

Formula for men

For men born before 2000, the formula looks like this:

$$Gua = 10 — S$$

For those born in 2000 and later:

$$Gua = 9 — S$$

Formula for women

For women before 2000:

$$Gua = 5 + S$$

For those born in 2000 and later:

$$Gua = 6 + S$$

Important clarification: In the Ba-Zhai system, the number 5 does not exist. If the calculation results in 5, then for men it is replaced by 2 (Earth), and for women - on 8 (Earth). Our calculator performs this replacement automatically.

Gua Number Groups

All people are divided into two energy groups. Each group has its own cardinal directions:

  • Eastern group (numbers 1, 3, 4, 9): Your best directions are North, South, East and Southeast.
  • Western group (numbers 2, 6, 7, 8): West, North-West, South-West and North-East are ideal for you.

The meaning of favorable directions

After the calculation, the calculator will give you a list of sectors. Here's what they mean in your life:

Favorable sectors (use for sleep and work)

  • Sheng Qi (Success): The most powerful direction. Ideal for doing business, active work and raising money.
  • Tian-I (Health): "Heavenly Healer" The head of the bed should be directed here if you need to restore strength or overcome an illness.
  • Yan-Nian (Love): Sector of long-term relationships and family harmony. Helps to establish connections and find a partner.
  • Fu Wei (Stability): Great for meditation and inner focus. Gives a clear mind and peace of mind.

Unfavorable sectors (what to avoid)

  • Ho-Hai: Minor failures, quarrels and annoying interruptions.
  • Wu Gui (5 spirits): Risk of theft, intrigue and fire.
  • Liu-Sha (6 assassins): Problems in legal matters and family scandals.
  • Jue Ming (Collapse): The most dangerous direction. Try not to spend a lot of time in this sector and do not sleep with your head in this direction.

Practical tips for use

For the calculator to be of real benefit, apply the data obtained in the interior:

  • Workplace: Place the table so that you are sitting facing your Sheng Qi or Fu Wei. This will increase productivity several times.
  • Dream: The head of the bed should face one of your four good directions. If the bed is in the “bad” sector, try to at least point the top of your head in the “good” direction.
  • Entrance door: It is ideal if the entrance to the house or apartment matches your group. If you are a person of the Western group, and the door faces the East, the elements of your element in the hallway will help smooth out the negativity.
