How does the calculator perform calculations?

Our tool uses classic mathematical formulas to work with fractions. Understanding these formulas will help you check calculations manually.

1. Converting mixed fractions

The first step is to convert a mixed fraction of the form $A \frac{B}{C}$ (Integer, Numerator, Denominator) into an improper fraction $\frac{N}{C}$ .

Formula:

$$N = (A \cdot C) + B$$

Example: $2 \frac{3}{4} = \frac{(2 \cdot 4) + 3}{4} = \frac{11}{4}$

Our calculator also handles negative integer parts correctly by applying the sign to the resulting numerator.

2. Basic arithmetic operations

For two fractions $\frac{a}{b}$ and $\frac{c}{d}$ the following rules apply:

  • Addition and Subtraction (Common Denominator)Addition Formula: $$\frac{a}{b} + \frac{c}{d} = \frac{ad + bc}{bd}$$ Subtraction Formula: $$\frac{a}{b} — \frac{c}{d} = \frac{ad — bc}{bd}$$
  • MultiplicationWhen multiplying, the numerators and denominators are simply multiplied: $$\frac{a}{b} \times \frac{c}{d} = \frac{ac}{bd}$$
  • Division (“Reversal” of the second fraction) Division is the same as multiplication by the reciprocal (reversed) fraction: $$\frac{a}{b} \div \frac{c}{d} = \frac{a}{b} \times \frac{d}{c} = \frac{ad}{bc}$$

3. Simplification (Reduction) of Fractions

Getting results is only half the battle. It needs to be simplified. Our calculator does this by finding Greatest Common Divisor (GCD) for the numerator ( $N$ ) and denominator ( $D$ ).

Simplification formula:

$$\text{Результат} = \frac{N \div \text{НОД}(N, D)}{D \div \text{НОД}(N, D)}$$

For example, if the result is $\frac{18}{24}$ :

  • $\text{НОД}(18, 24) = 6$
  • Simplified fraction = $\frac{18 \div 6}{24 \div 6} = \frac{3}{4}$
