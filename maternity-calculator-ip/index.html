Maternity payments calculator for individual entrepreneurs: calculation of benefits for BiR

Individual entrepreneurs, unlike employees, do not automatically receive maternity benefits (M&B). To do this, you must enroll in the program in advance. voluntary social insurance and pay contributions to the Social Fund of Russia (SFR).

The main rule: to be eligible for payment, the insurance premium must be paid in full before December 31, 2024. If you are planning a maternity leave in 2026, the contribution must be made before the end of the current 2025.

Conditions for receiving benefits under BiR

  • Registration with the SFR: Availability of a voluntary insurance agreement.
  • Timely payment: The contribution for the previous calendar year has been paid in full.
  • IP status: At the time of the occurrence of an insured event (opening of sick leave), the activities of the individual entrepreneur should not be terminated.

Formula for calculating maternity benefits for individual entrepreneurs

Since the individual entrepreneur pays a fixed contribution, the amount of his income does not affect the amount of the benefit. The payment is strictly tied to Minimum wage established on the start date of maternity leave.

The calculation is made according to the following algorithm:

  1. Determination of average daily earnings: $$SDZ = \frac{MROT \times 24}{730}$$ Where $MROT$ is the minimum wage at the time of maternity leave; 24 - number of months in two accounting years; 730 is a fixed number of days.
  2. Total payment amount: $$Total = SDZ \times Days \times RK$$ Where $Days$ is the duration of sick leave; $RK$ - regional coefficient (if applicable).

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to pay taxes on maternity payments?

No, maternity benefits are not subject to personal income tax and you do not have to pay insurance premiums. This is the net income of the entrepreneur.

Is it possible to work during an individual entrepreneur's maternity leave?

Yes, the law does not prohibit an individual entrepreneur from continuing to operate and receive income from the business while receiving benefits under the BiR. The SFR has no right to deprive payments for this.

What if the minimum wage changes in the middle of maternity leave?

The benefit amount is calculated once on the date of opening of sick leave. A subsequent increase in the minimum wage usually does not entail a recalculation of the already assigned payment.
