Mathematical calculation model

The calculator is based on the annuity payment formula. This is a method in which you pay the bank in equal amounts every month throughout the term.

The monthly payment is calculated using the following formula:

$$A = P \times \frac{r(1+r)^n}{(1+r)^n — 1}$$

Where:

  • $A$ - final monthly payment;
  • $P$ - loan body (cost of housing minus down payment and capital);
  • $r$ is the monthly interest rate, which is calculated as $\frac{0.06}{12} = 0.005$ ;
  • $n$ - total number of months (loan term in years $\times 12$ ).

How to reduce your mortgage overpayment

Even with a preferential rate of 6%, overpayment over a long horizon (20-30 years) can be significant. To minimize the total cost of housing, experts recommend:

  1. Increase the down payment: Every ruble invested at the start saves up to 2-3 rubles in interest in the future.
  2. Use maternity capital immediately: Directing a certificate to repay the principal debt at the beginning of the term is more effective than at the end.
  3. Choose the optimal time: The difference in payment between 20 and 30 years is often insignificant, but the overpayment at 30 years increases like an avalanche.
