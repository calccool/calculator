Mathematical calculation formulas

For those who want to understand the working principle or check the calculations manually, we present the basic formulas used in our algorithm.

1. Calculating the percentage of a number

To find the part ( $V$ ) knowing the total ( $Total$ ) and percentage ( $P$ ):

$$V = Total \times \frac{P}{100}$$

2. Calculation of percentage

To find out what percentage a part ( $Part$ ) is of a whole ( $Total$ ):

$$P = \frac{Part}{Total} \times 100\%$$

3. Calculation of the fraction of a number

To find the value of the fraction expressed as a fraction $\frac{A}{B}$ of a number ( $Total$ ):

$$V = Total \times \frac{A}{B}$$
