Tarkov Flea Market Commission Calculator: Tax and Profit Calculation (Flea Market Fee)

Trading between players in Escape from Tarkov is an art of balance. Unlike regular stores, Flea Market charges a dynamic tax, which depends not only on the transaction amount, but also on the “intrinsic” value of the item. Our calculator allows you to find out in advance how many rubles you will receive in your hands, and whether it is worth listing the item at an inflated price.

The main trap for a beginner is geometric growth of commission. If the sale price deviates too much from the system (base) price, the tax may exceed the amount of the sale itself, turning the transaction into a net loss.

Tax calculation formula (Flea Market Fee)

The mathematical model of the commission in EFT is based on decimal logarithms and exponentiation. The complete formula our tool uses is as follows:

$$Fee = (V_0 \times 0.05 \times 4^{\log_{10}(V_0 / V_1)} + V_1 \times 0.05 \times 4^{\log_{10}(V_1 / V_0)}) \times Mod$$

Where variables mean:

  • $V_0$ — The base price of the item (Base Price), set by the developers.
  • $V_1$ - The price at which you list the item for sale.
  • $Mod$ - Bonus multiplier for your Vault (Intelligence Center).

Basic parameters for calculation

To get an accurate result, you need to fill in three key fields:

  1. Base Price: This is a hidden parameter. The easiest way to find out is to look at the selling price of the item to the Therapist or Mechanic (it is usually 40-60% of the base).
  2. Specified price: The amount in rubles that other players will see in the list of lots.
  3. Intelligence center: A module in the Vault that directly reduces your costs.

Why is it important to use a calculator?

Using automated calculation gives the player several strategic advantages:

  • Avoiding Overprice: The game algorithm punishes attempts to sell cheap things (for example, keys or modules) at extremely high prices. The calculator will highlight such transactions in red.
  • Optimization of packs (Bulk Selling): When selling ammunition or medicine in large quantities, even a 1% difference in tax turns into hundreds of thousands of rubles saved.
  • Cost-benefit analysis: Our interface includes a visual dashboard “Profitability Analysis”, which in real time shows the share of tax in the total turnover of the transaction.

How to find out the Base Price of an item?

Since Battlestate Games does not display the base price in the inventory interface, players use indirect methods:

  • Comparison with traders: The therapist purchases items for approximately 60–63% of their base cost.
  • External APIs: Specialized resources (Tarkov-Market, etc.) parse data from game files, providing the exact valuebasePrice.

Tips for profitable trading

  1. Monitor your margins: If the commission takes up more than 30% of the item price, try lowering the price a little. You will often get more net profit by listing the item cheaper.
  2. Upgrade the Intelligence Center: At the 3rd level of the module, you save almost a third on each exhibited lot, which is critically important with turnover of tens of millions.
  3. Check the lots before clicking “Submit”: Always check the number in the “Total payable” field in the game with the calculations of the calculator to prevent an accidental error of an extra zero.
