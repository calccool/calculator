CPL and ROAS calculator: Online calculation of advertising payback and unit economics

It is critical for every digital marketer and business owner to understand where the advertising budget goes and what kind of return it brings. This calculator allows you to instantly calculate key performance metrics (KPIs), ranging from the cost of one click to net profit, taking into account the margin of your product.

The tool is based on the principle of transparency of the sales funnel. You can use both Basic Analytics for quick cost estimation and the advanced Unit Economics mode for in-depth analysis of return on investment.

Key metrics and funnel stages

The effectiveness of an advertising campaign is assessed at different levels of user interaction:

  • Traffic: Volume of attracted users (clicks).
  • Lead generation: The process of converting visitors into potential clients (applications).
  • Sales: The final stage of converting a lead into a paid order.
  • Profitability: Ratio of revenue to budget spent.

Formulas for calculating marketing indicators

To gain a deep understanding of processes, we use classic financial analysis formulas. All calculations are performed in real time.

  1. Cost per lead ( $CPL$ ): $$CPL = \frac{Budget}{Leads}$$ Where $Budget$ is the total advertising costs, and $Leads$ is the number of applications received.
  2. Return on Advertising Spend ( $ROAS$ ): $$ROAS = \frac{Revenue}{Budget} \cdot 100\%$$ Shows gross income for each invested ruble.
  3. Return on Marketing Investment ( $ROMI$ ): $$ROMI = \frac{(Revenue \cdot Margin) — Budget}{Budget} \cdot 100\%$$ The most accurate indicator that takes into account business margins and real net profit.

Advanced calculator features

The tool is equipped with interactive modules that make the analysis visual:

  • Funnel visualization: Graphic display of the “narrowing” of the customer flow from click to purchase, allowing you to find the “bottleneck” in sales.
  • Payback indicator (Donut Gauge): Color indication of business status. If your advertising is running at a loss, the system will highlight the result in red.
  • Marketer's Dictionary: Built-in tips for each metric (CPC, CPL, CPA), available in one click.
  • Calculation of net profit: Automatically subtract the cost of goods and services from total revenue to obtain real income figures.

How to interpret the results

If your ROAS is 500%, which means that for every ruble spent you receive 5 rubles in revenue. However, this does not guarantee profit if your product margins are low. This is why we recommend switching to mode "Unit Economics", where the calculation goes through ROMI. If your ROMI is above 0%, your marketing is making real money after all purchasing or production costs are taken into account.

Use this calculator to regularly monitor advertising channels (Yandex.Direct, targeted advertising, seeding) in order to turn off ineffective ads in a timely manner and scale profitable combinations.
