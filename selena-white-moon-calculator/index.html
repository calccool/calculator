Mathematical algorithm for calculating Selena

Selena is not a physical body, it is a mathematical point associated with the movement of the lunar perigee. She moves throughout the Zodiac evenly, completing a full circle in exactly 7 years.

The formula for the average movement of a fictitious point relative to the reference epoch (Standard Epoch) is used for calculation.

Position Calculation Formula

Selene's position in degrees ( $\theta$ ) is calculated as follows:

$$\theta = \left( \frac{T_{birth} — T_{epoch}}{T_{cycle}} \cdot 360^\circ \right) \pmod{360}$$

Where:

  • $T_{birth}$ — time of birth in milliseconds or days.
  • $T_{epoch}$ is the reference point (this calculator uses July 14, 2001, when Selena entered 0° Aries).
  • $T_{cycle}$ - full orbital period equal to $2556.84$ earthly days (7 years).

The resulting $\theta$ value determines the Zodiac sign. For example, if the result is in the range from $0^\circ$ to $30^\circ$ - this is Aries, from $30^\circ$ to $60^\circ$ - Taurus, and so on.
