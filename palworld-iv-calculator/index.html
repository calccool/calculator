Calculator IV in Palworld: Calculate hidden stats (genes) of Palov

In Palworld, two creatures of the same type and level can have completely different characteristics. The reason for this lies in IV (Individual Values) - hidden “genes” or individual meanings. These parameters determine the genetic potential of your companion and directly affect the final indicators of health, damage and survivability.

Each Pal has three independent genes:

  • IV HP (Health)
  • IV Attack (Attack)
  • IV Defense (Protection)

Values ​​range from 0 to 100. A fell with an IV of 100 receives a 30% bonus to the base growth of characteristics compared to an individual whose IV is 0. Our calculator allows you to instantly conduct a “genome analysis” and understand whether it is worth spending resources on leveling up a particular individual or whether it is better to use it for further crossing.

How to use the hidden stats calculator

To obtain an accurate IV test result, follow these instructions:

Step 1: Selecting the type and base

You can choose a popular Pal (for example, Jetragon or Anubis ) from the drop-down list or switch to the “Manual database entry” mode if you found data on the basic characteristics of a new mod or rare creature on Wikipedia.

Step 2: Enter current indicators

Enter your Pal's current level (from 1 to 50) and its actual stats that you see in the game menu.

Step 3: Account for Passive Skills

This is a critical point. If your Pal has passive skills such as Ferocious (+20% attack) or Legend (+20% attack and defense), be sure to indicate the total bonus percentage in the appropriate fields. The calculator will automatically “clean” the bonuses to calculate the pure genetic code.

Important: Before calculating, make sure that Pal is not under the influence of stat-boosting food or boosted through the Pal Essence Condenser, as this may skew the results.

Mathematical model for calculating IV

The calculator uses inverse calculation algorithms to determine hidden genes. First, “pure” characteristics are determined without taking into account passive skills:

$$PureStat = \frac{CurrentStat}{1 + \frac{Bonus\%}{100}}$$

Then the position of the current stat between the theoretical minimum (IV 0) and maximum (IV 100) for a given level is calculated using the formula:

$$IV = \frac{Stat_{fact} — Stat_{min}}{Stat_{max} — Stat_{min}} \times 100$$

Where $Stat_{min}$ is the base stat at the current level with zero genetic bonus.

Why is it important to know IV when breeding?

Breeding is the key to creating the strongest creatures in Palworld. When crossed, parents have a chance to pass on their high IVs to their offspring.

  1. Inheritance: If both parents have Attack IV equal to 100, the probability of getting an egg with the same indicator increases significantly.
  2. Resource saving: Leveling Pal to level 50 costs a huge amount of Pal Souls and time. Knowledge of IV allows you to invest resources only in those who will show maximum efficiency at the cap.
  3. Minimum requirements: To defeat the most difficult bosses (for example, Frostallion or Necromus), it is recommended to have fellas with a rank of at least A.
