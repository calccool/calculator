Deposit tax calculator: calculation of personal income tax and tax-free base at the Central Bank rate

According to current legislation, not the entire deposit amount and not even all interest received are subject to tax. Only the total interest income from all your accounts and deposits in Russian banks for the calendar year, which exceeds the “tax-free minimum” established by law, is taken into account.

Our calculator takes into account the key parameter - maximum key rate of the Central Bank for the reporting year. This allows you to get the most accurate result that corresponds to the Federal Tax Service methodology.

Formula for calculating the tax-free limit

The tax-free base (limit) is calculated according to a single standard for all citizens, regardless of the amount of their savings.

$$L = 1\,000\,000 \times R_{max}$$

Where:

  • $L$ - tax-free amount of income (in rubles);
  • $1\,000\,000$ - fixed base of one million rubles;
  • $R_{max}$ is the maximum value of the Bank of Russia key rate that was in effect on the 1st day of each month during the tax period.

Calculation of the amount of tax to be paid

If your annual income on all deposits is above the $L$ limit, then the $T$ tax is calculated as follows:

$$T = (I_{total} — L) \times Rate_{tax}$$

Where:

  • $I_{total}$ - your total income on all deposits for the year;
  • $Rate_{tax}$ - personal income tax rate (13% in the base case or 15% with total annual income over 5 million rubles).

Important nuances of taxation

  • Currency deposits: Income on them is recalculated into rubles at the Central Bank exchange rate on the date of actual receipt of interest.
  • Exception accounts: Tax is not paid on income from escrow accounts and ruble accounts, the rate on which throughout the year did not exceed 1% per annum.
  • Cashback: Bonuses and cashback from banks are generally not considered interest income and are not subject to this tax.
  • Increased rate 15%: If the sum of all your income (salary, dividends, interest on deposits, etc.) for the year exceeds 5 million rubles, a 15% rate is applied to the excess amount.

Tax payment deadlines in 2025

  • Until February 1: Banks transmit information about your income to the Federal Tax Service.
  • Autumn: The tax service sends a notification to the taxpayer’s personal account (or by mail).
  • Until December 1: Deadline for paying taxes for the previous year.
