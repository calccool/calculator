Cayo Perico Heist Calculator: Calculation of Shares and Profits in GTA Online

Heisting Cayo Perico remains the most lucrative way to make money in GTA Online, but the total amount in your wallet is always less than what you see in the bags on the island. The system automatically deducts the share of support and intermediaries.

Mandatory commissions

Before the money is distributed among team members, two main expenses are subtracted from the “dirty” jackpot:

  1. Buyer's commission (10%): Fees for laundering gold, money and other valuables.
  2. Pavel's share (2%): Costs for maintaining the killer whale submarine and the services of your faithful assistant.

In total, the total commission is 12% from total production.

Formula for calculating net income

The following mathematical model is used to determine the final payout for each player:

$$P_{final} = \left( T_{total} \times 0.88 \times \frac{S_{player}}{100} \right) + B_{elite}$$

Where:

  • $T_{total}$ - total amount of loot in bags;
  • $S_{player}$ - percentage share of a specific player;
  • $B_{elite}$ - bonus for completing the Elite Challenge.

Bonuses and difficulty levels

The difficulty level affects not only the number of lives and fingerprint hacking, but also the size of the bonus for the Elite Challenge. It is important to remember that this bonus is paid to everyone to the player separately and is not subject to taxes from Paul or the buyer.
