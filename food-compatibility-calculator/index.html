The main principle of compatibility can be described through the chemistry of enzymes.

  1. Proteins (meat, fish, eggs) require the enzyme pepsin to break down, which only works in an acidic environment. $$Protein \xrightarrow{Acid (Pepsin)} Amino Acids$$

Conflict: If you eat Protein and Starch at the same time (for example, a cutlet with potatoes), the acidic environment of the stomach secreted for meat instantly neutralizes the alkaline enzyme ptyalin. $$Acid + Alkali \rightarrow Neutralization \rightarrow Fermentation$$ As a result, starches are not digested, but ferment, and proteins begin to rot.
