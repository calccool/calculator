Transport tax calculator for special equipment: tractors, cranes, excavators

Owners of self-propelled vehicles and specialized vehicles are required to pay transport tax annually. Unlike passenger cars, calculations for special equipment (tractors, excavators, truck cranes) have their own specific rates, which strongly depend on the category of the vehicle and its power.

Our calculator allows you to instantly calculate the amount of payment to the budget, based on current tax parameters.

Tax calculation formula

Tax calculation is based on the product of engine power, tax rate and period of ownership of the equipment during the calendar year. If your region provides benefits, the total amount is reduced by the discount amount.

Mathematically, the calculation looks like this:

$$T = P \times R \times \frac{m}{12} \times (1 — \frac{D}{100})$$

Where:

  • $T$ — total amount of tax payable (RUB);
  • $P$ — engine power in horsepower ( hp );
  • $R$ — tax rate established by the constituent entity of the Russian Federation (rub./hp);
  • $m$ — the number of complete months of equipment ownership per year;
  • $D$ — the size of the regional benefit (in percentage).

Instructions for using the calculator

To get an accurate result, follow these steps:

  1. Select type of equipment: Switch the tab between “Tractors” (for agricultural and construction equipment) or “Trucks” (for truck cranes and truck-based equipment).
  2. Enter power: Enter the data from the PSM (self-propelled vehicle passport) or PTS. For popular models such as MTZ-80, you can use the quick entry buttons.
  3. Check the rate: The calculator will enter the average automatically, but you can edit it manually according to the law in your region.
  4. Specify the holding period: If the equipment was purchased or sold in the current year, select the appropriate number of months.
  5. Apply benefits: If you qualify for a reduced rate or tax exemption, activate the Regional Benefit radio button and enter the discount percentage.

Features of taxation of special equipment

It is important to consider that not all special equipment is subject to tax. There are exceptions that can completely exempt the owner from payments:

  • Agricultural machinery: Tractors and combines used directly for the production of agricultural products are not subject to taxation under certain conditions.
  • Stolen equipment: If a vehicle is wanted, tax is not charged for it if there are supporting certificates.
  • Regional preferences: In a number of constituent entities of the Russian Federation, there are benefits for organizations involved in road construction or transportation.
