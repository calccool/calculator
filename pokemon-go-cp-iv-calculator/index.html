Basic calculation formulas

To calculate the final indicators, mathematical models are used that take into account Base Stats, individual IV values ​​and the level multiplier.

Combat Power Formula:

$$CP = \lfloor \frac{(BaseAtk + IvAtk) \cdot \sqrt{BaseDef + IvDef} \cdot \sqrt{BaseSta + IvSta} \cdot CPM^2}{10} \rfloor$$

Hit Points formula:

$$HP = \max(10, \lfloor (BaseSta + IvSta) \cdot CPM \rfloor)$$

Where $CPM$ is a coefficient that increases with each level of the Pokemon. For example, at level 40 it is approximately 0.7903, and at the maximum level 50 it reaches 0.8403.

Deciphering levels IV

  1. Attack: Affects the damage dealt by fast and charged attacks. A high rate is critical for raids.
  2. Defense: Reduces damage received from the enemy.
  3. Stamina (HP): Determines the total amount of health.

In the “Stat Evaluation” mode, the calculator sums up all the values. The maximum possible amount is 45 units 15/15/15. If your score is above 37, the Pokemon receives three stars, and at 45, it receives a red seal of “perfection.”

Why is 0/15/15 better for PvP?

In Leagues with a CP cap of 1500 or 2500, attack contributes the most to the total. By reducing the attack to a minimum, you allow the Pokemon to reach a higher level while remaining within the limit. This gives a significant advantage in survivability and overall damage output to the Stat Product.
