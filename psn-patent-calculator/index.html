Patent cost calculator for individual entrepreneurs 2026: calculation of PSN online

The patent tax system (PTS) remains one of the most attractive for small businesses in Russia due to its predictability and the absence of complex reporting. However, the final tax amount does not depend on actual revenue, but on potential annual income ( PVGD ), established by regional authorities.

Our calculator allows you to instantly calculate not only the base cost, but also the total amount payable, taking into account legal tax optimization.

Basic calculation parameters

To obtain an accurate result, three key factors must be taken into account:

  • Regional PVGD: The amount of income that the region “allows” to be earned from this type of activity.
  • Validity: A patent can be obtained for any period from 1 to 12 months (within a calendar year).
  • Tax rate: The standard rate is 6%, but in a number of regions (for example, Crimea, Sevastopol, or within the framework of “tax holidays”) it may be lower.

Formula for calculating PSN

Calculation of the cost of a patent is based on the following mathematical model:

$$S = \frac{PVGD}{D_y} \times D_p \times R$$

Where:

  • $S$ - total patent value;
  • $PVGD$ - potential annual income;
  • $D_y$ - number of days in a year (365 or 366);
  • $D_p$ - the number of days for which the patent is taken;
  • $R$ - tax rate (in decimal form, for example, 0.06).

Reducing the cost of a patent for insurance premiums

The main advantage of PSN in 2026 is the ability to legally reduce the tax to zero at the expense of paid insurance premiums for yourself and your employees.

Patent payment terms

The payment algorithm depends on the period for which the document was issued. If your patent is valid for more than 6 months, the payment is divided into two parts:

Payment schedule for long-term patents

  1. First payment (1/3 of the amount): Must be paid within the first 90 calendar days from the start of the patent.
  2. Second payment (2/3 of the amount): Paid until the patent expiration date.

If the patent is taken for a term up to 6 months, the entire amount (100%) is paid in one payment until the end of its validity period.

Restrictions on the use of PSN

Before calculating, make sure your business meets the criteria of the patent system:

  • The number of employees does not exceed 15 people.
  • Annual income for all patents no more than 60 million rubles.
  • The type of activity is permitted for PSN in your region.

Important: If you combine the STS with the simplified taxation system (STS), the income limit of 60 million rubles is calculated in total for both regimes.
