Room area calculator - calculate the area in m² quickly and accurately

Online room area calculator allows you to accurately calculate the area of ​​the room in m², determine the perimeter, the length of the plinth, the area of ​​the walls taking into account openings, and even add a reserve for materials. A convenient tool for repairs, planning and calculating the amount of finishing materials - it works instantly and is suitable for rooms of any shape.

Why calculate the area of ​​a room?

When renovating, decorating or buying furniture, it is important to know the exact dimensions of the room. The online calculator will help:

  • Calculate the amount of paint, wallpaper, tiles, laminate or linoleum.
  • Plan a budget without overpaying for unnecessary materials.
  • Select furniture and equipment to suit the actual size of the room.
  • Determine the length of the plinth, the perimeter and area of ​​the walls to be finished.

You no longer need to calculate manually - the calculator will do all the calculations in seconds.

How to use the room area calculator

  1. Choose the shape of the room - rectangular, L-shaped, round, trapezoidal or freeform.
  2. Enter length, width or other parameters (depending on the shape).
  3. Specify the height of the walls if you need to calculate the area for wallpaper or painting.
  4. Add the total area of ​​openings (windows, doors) and the percentage of margin for trimming.
  5. Click "Calculate" — the system will automatically provide results in m² and meters.

Calculation of the area of ​​complex shapes

The calculator supports different types of premises:

  • Rectangular — enter length and width.
  • L-shaped — you can add or subtract two zones.
  • Round — calculation by diameter or radius.
  • Trapezoid - base A, base B and height.
  • Free-form (polygonal) — input of point coordinates for complex layouts.

Additional functions of the calculator:

  • Calculation perimeter And skirting board length.
  • Definition wall area in height.
  • Accounting openings (windows, doors) when calculating walls.
  • Addition stock of materials (5–15%).
  • Opportunity copy the result in one click.
  • Support all types of premises no manual formulas.

Frequently asked questions

❓ How to calculate the area of ​​a room with niches or protrusions?

Add the parameters of each zone separately - the calculator summarizes the total area.

❓ Can this calculator be used to calculate tiles or laminate?

Yes, include a margin of 10–15% - the result will show the required amount of coverage, taking into account trimming.

❓ How to calculate the area of ​​walls for wallpaper?

Specify the height of the ceiling and the perimeter of the room, the calculator will automatically take into account the openings and give the exact area for gluing.
