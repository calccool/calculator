Mana and health reservation calculator in PoE 3.25+: Calculate aura retention

Planning a build in Path of Exile (PoE) is impossible without accurate calculations retention (reservation) of resources. With the introduction of the “Reservation Efficiency” mechanic, calculation “by eye” became almost impossible due to non-linear progression. Our tool allows you to instantly calculate how much mana or health your character will have left after activating all the necessary auras.

How to use the calculator

To get an accurate result, follow these steps:

  1. Select mode: switch between "Mana Reserve" and "Health Reserve" (for builds through Arrogance / Arrogance or Blood Magic ).
  2. Enter pool volume: your maximum amount of mana or health without taking into account retention.
  3. Specify Efficiency: the total value from the tree of passive skills, equipment properties and gems.
  4. Add auras: enter a base percentage (for example, 50% for Determination ) or flat value (for Clarity or Precision ).
  5. Select multiplier: indicate the level of the support stone “Mentor” (Enlighten) or “Arrogance” (Arrogance).

Reservation calculation formula

In the modern version of PoE, the calculation is based not on subtracting percentages, but on increasing the “capacity” of the resource. Each point of efficiency allows auras to take up less space in your pool.

Mathematically this is expressed by the following formula:

$$Reserved = \lceil \frac{BaseCost \times Multiplier}{1 + \frac{Efficiency}{100}} \rceil$$

Where:

  • BaseCost — basic aura retention (in % or units).
  • Multiplier — support stone multiplier (Enlighten, Arrogance).
  • Efficiency — your overall reservation efficiency.
  • $\lceil \dots \rceil$ — rounding up (ceil), since PoE always rounds the retained resource up.

Why is Efficiency better than old cost cutting?

Previously, PoE used "Reduced Reservation", which allowed to achieve 100% reduction and make auras free. Current system Mana Reservation Efficiency works differently:

  • 100% Efficiency means that the auras are in 2 times less (you can launch twice as many auras).
  • 200% Efficiency means that the auras are in 3 times less.
  • This eliminates the possibility of making auras completely free, but it does provide a predictable and fair progression for any build.

Mana Optimization Tips

  • Mana Mastery: Be sure to take the 12% efficiency node if you pass nearby.
  • Small Cluster Gems: Can provide a huge boost to effectiveness for specific types of auras (for example, for ailments or protection).
  • Effect on equipment: Helmets and shields with the influence of the Creator or Redeemer often have suffixes on the effectiveness of the reservation.

Important: Our calculator takes into account Path of Exile's rounding rules. If after calculations you have 0 mana left, you will not be able to activate skills, even if the calculator shows that there is enough mana. Always leave 10-20 mana for using abilities through Lifetap or by reducing the cost of skills.
