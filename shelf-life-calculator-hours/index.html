How the calculation algorithm works

The mathematical model of the calculator is based on the addition of time stamps (Unix Timestamp). To calculate the expiration date, use the following formula:

$$T_{exp} = T_{prod} + (H \times 3600000)$$

Where:

  • $T_{exp}$ - final time and expiration date;
  • $T_{prod}$ — production (production) time and date;
  • $H$ — set storage period in hours;
  • $3600000$ — clock conversion factor to milliseconds.

For the Freshness Control function, the percentage of wear (elapsed time) is calculated relative to the current moment:

$$P = \frac{T_{now} — T_{prod}}{T_{exp} — T_{prod}} \times 100\%$$

If the received value is $P \ge 100\%$ , the product is considered expired and is visualized with a red safety status.
