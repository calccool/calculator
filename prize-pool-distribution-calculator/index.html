Prize fund calculator: distribution of places in the online tournament

Organizing any competitive event—from a local chess tournament to a large-scale eSports event—requires competent financial planning. One of the most difficult tasks is the fair and mathematically sound distribution of monetary rewards.

Our calculator allows you to instantly calculate the shares of each participant using time-tested algorithms. The tool takes into account not only the number of prizes, but also possible deductions of platform commissions or taxes, which guarantees the accuracy of payouts in person.

  • Quick copy: Results for each location can be copied in one click.

Mathematical distribution models

To ensure fairness, we use several algorithms. The most popular is the “Esports Classic”, which is based on a power function. The weight of each $W_i$ seat is calculated using the formula:

$$W_i = \frac{1}{i^{1.2}}$$

Where $i$ is the serial number of the occupied space. The final payout amount for a specific $P_i$ location is determined as:

$$P_i = \frac{W_i}{\sum_{j=1}^{n} W_j} \times S_{net}$$

Where $S_{net}$ is the net prize pool after deducting all commissions.

How to use the calculator

  1. Enter Total prize fund in the appropriate field.
  2. Specify Number of seats, between whom the amount will be distributed.
  3. Choose the right one Distribution scheme in the drop-down list.
  4. If the tournament is brokered or taxed, turn on the toggle Take into account the commission and enter the percentage.
  5. Click the button Calculate to get a detailed statement and visualization.
