Probation end date calculator

Establishing a probationary period is a standard procedure for hiring, regulated Art. 70 Labor Code of the Russian Federation. However, the calculation of the final date often causes controversy: should the first day be counted? How do holidays affect? What to do if an employee is sick?

Our calculator automates this process using an “inclusive counting” algorithm that is legally valid for labor relations.

Basic calculation rules

According to the general rule, a period calculated in months expires on the corresponding date of the last month of the term. If the end of the period falls on a month in which there is no corresponding date, then the period expires on the last day of that month.

Mathematical formula for calculation:

$$D_{end} = D_{start} + T_{period} + D_{absent} — 1 \text{ день}$$

Where:

  • $D_{end}$ - last day of testing;
  • $D_{start}$ — start date of work (first working day);
  • $T_{period}$ — duration of the test (in months or days);
  • $D_{absent}$ — the number of calendar days of actual absence (sick leave, vacations at your own expense).

Who should not be given a probationary period?

There are categories of citizens for whom testing is prohibited by law:

  • Pregnant women and mothers of children under 1.5 years old.
  • Persons invited by transfer from another employer.
  • Minors (under 18 years of age).
  • Graduates of universities and colleges (for the first job in their specialty within a year).
  • Winners of the competition for the position.

How is the period extended in absence?

It is important to understand that the probationary period is a period actual work. If an employee is absent, the deadline is automatically advanced by the appropriate number of days.

The calculation of the period does not include:

  • Periods of temporary incapacity for work (sick leave).
  • Short-term leaves without pay.
  • Days of absenteeism or suspension from work.
  • Any other periods when the employee did not actually perform duties.

What to do if the last day is a day off?

If the end date of the test falls on a non-working day (Saturday, Sunday or holiday), then the end date is considered the next following working day (according to Article 14 of the Labor Code of the Russian Federation). However, in practice, the employer is recommended to decide on passing the test in advance, so as not to violate the notification procedure 3 days before the final.
