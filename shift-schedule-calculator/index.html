Shift schedule calculator 2/2, 3/3 and 1/3 for the year ahead

Planning personal time and budget during shift work requires precision. Our interactive tool allows you to instantly create a two-by-two, three-by-three, or three-day-by-three chart 365 days in advance. Unlike conventional grids, this calculator integrates the production calendar with financial planning.

Who is this tool useful for?

  • For employees with schedules 2/2 and 3/3: for accurate planning of vacations and weekends.
  • For daily shift workers (1/3): to track duty schedules.
  • HR managers and executives: to assess staff workload.
  • For freelancers and self-employed: to calculate the potential profit for project employment.

How to use the calculator

The interface is built on the principle of progressive data disclosure. To get accurate results, follow three simple steps:

  1. Select chart type: switch between the 2/2, 3/3 or 1/3 tabs at the top of the block.
  2. Specify a starting point: Select the date of your first shift in the calendar.
  3. Set up payment options: Enter the duration of the shift in hours and your rate to activate the financial block.

Mathematical model and calculation formulas

The calculator algorithm is based on cyclic progression. We calculate each of the 365 days of the year, determining its status based on the remainder of the date difference divided by the cycle length.

Key indicators

To generate a financial forecast, the following variables and formulas are used:

  • L (Cycle Length): Length of the cycle (for example, for 2/2 it is $L = 2 + 2 = 4$ ).
  • D (Days Diff): Number of days from the start of the first shift.
  • W (Work Shifts): Total number of work shifts in a year.
  • R (Rate): Rate per shift.

Formula for total income for the year ( $I_{total}$ ):

$$I_{total} = W \times R$$

Average monthly income formula ( $I_{avg}$ ):

$$I_{avg} = \frac{W \times R}{12}$$

Features and benefits of our service

We have implemented advanced UX/UI solutions to make interaction with the calculator as productive as possible.

Interactive visualization

The calculator doesn’t just give out numbers, it builds a visual model of your employment:

  • Pie Chart: Shows the ratio of work and rest in the annual cycle.
  • Smart calendar: Highlights work shifts in blue, allowing you to switch between months for detailed planning.
  • Instant calculation: All parameter changes (date, rate, hours) are applied on the fly without reloading the page.

Financial forecasting

Enter the rate per shift $(\text{в поле «Ставка за смену»})$ and the system will automatically calculate:

  • Total income for 12 months.
  • Average salary per month (convenient for comparison with a fixed salary).
  • The total number of man-hours worked for the year.

Planning Guidelines

Use the obtained data to optimize your life. For example, knowing the schedule for the year ahead, you can:

  • Book vacation tickets on dates where work shifts fall on holidays.
  • Distribute the financial burden, understanding in which months there will be more work shifts.
  • Avoid burnout by visually assessing schedule density.

Important: The calculation is predictive and does not take into account possible adjustments to the schedule by the employer, holiday transfers (if they are not provided for in the contract) and overtime payments.
