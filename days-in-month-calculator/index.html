How many days are there in a month? Calculator-calendar

In the Gregorian calendar, which is used by most countries in the world, the number of days in a month is not constant. It ranges from 28 to 31 days, which often causes confusion when planning, payroll, rent or project deadlines.

Our online days and months calculator solves this problem instantly. All you have to do is enter the year, and the tool will show the exact length of each of the 12 months, and also determine whether the year is a leap year.

How are the days distributed in a year?

A standard calendar year consists of 365 days, and a leap year has 366 days. The days are distributed among the months as follows:

  • 31 days: January, March, May, July, August, October, December.
  • 30 days: April, June, September, November.
  • 28 or 29 days: February.

The Mystery of February: Leap Year

The most difficult part of the calendar is February. Its duration depends on whether the year is a leap year.

  • Normal year (365 days): February has 28 days.
  • Leap year (366 days): February has 29 days.

Leap year calculation rule

Our calculator uses an accurate astronomical algorithm to determine leap years. A year is considered a leap year if one of the following conditions is met:

  1. The year is divisible by 4 without a remainder, BUT is not divisible by 100.
  2. The year is divisible by 400 without a remainder.

Examples:

  • 2024 — Leap year (divisible by 4).
  • 2025 - Ordinary.
  • 1900 — Normal (divisible by 4 and 100, but not by 400).
  • 2000 — Leap year (divisible by 400).
