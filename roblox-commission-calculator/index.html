Roblox commission calculator: accurate calculation of Robux for gamepasses and clothes

Every developer or content creator on Roblox faces the platform's systemic tax. When selling gamepasses ( Gamepasses ), clothing or access to places, the service retains a fixed share. In order not to make mistakes in calculations and not to set a price that is too low, it is necessary to understand the internal economics of transactions.

Basic rule 70/30

The platform has a standard distribution of funds:

  • 70% — the creator receives (your net profit);
  • 30% — Roblox retains (commission for using infrastructure, hosting and promotion).

Our tool is not just a gamepass commission calculator, but a full-fledged analytical panel that allows you to take into account mass sales and instantly convert earned Robux into US dollars at the current exchange rate DevEx.

Mathematical calculation model

In order for the calculations to be as accurate as possible, we use two basic formulas. They help determine either your revenue or the required cost of goods in the store.

Calculation of profit from a known price

If you have already set the price in the store, your net profit is calculated using the formula:

$$Profit = \lfloor Price \times 0.7 \rfloor$$

The round down function is used here since Roblox does not award fractional parts of Robux.

Price calculation to obtain the desired amount

If your goal is to get a specific amount of currency on your balance (for example, to pay an assistant’s salary or for personal needs), the price formula will be as follows:

$$Price = \lceil \frac{Desired\ Profit}{0.7} \rceil$$

In this case, rounding up is applied so that after deducting 30% tax, your account remains at least the required amount.
