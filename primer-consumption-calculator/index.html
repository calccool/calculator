Primer consumption calculator per m²

Primer consumption calculator per m² will help you calculate how much primer will be needed to treat the surface before painting or plastering.
The tool is suitable for professional builders and home craftsmen - just specify the type of primer, area, number of layers and additional parameters.

Main features of the calculator:

  • Calculation by area or by wall dimensions (length × height).
  • Accounting absorption coefficient for different types of surfaces (concrete, plaster, brick, wood).
  • Settings number of layers and percentage of losses.
  • Converting mass to liters by density primer composition.
  • Calculation required number of packages and approximate cost.

How to use the calculator

  1. Select the type of primer from the list (deep-penetrating, acrylic, etc.).
  2. Indicate the area or dimensions of the wall.
  3. Enter the number of layers, losses and, if necessary, density.
  4. Click “Calculate” - the calculator will show the total consumption in kg and liters, as well as the number of containers.

Approximate primer consumption rates

Good to know

  • How more porous base, the higher the consumption.
  • When applied in two layers consumption is multiplied by 2.
  • When using a roller or spray gun, losses can reach 10–15 %.
  • The average density of most primers is 1 kg/l, which is convenient for converting to liters.

Primer calculator is a convenient tool that will help you avoid unnecessary costs and accurately calculate the required amount of material for any construction task.
