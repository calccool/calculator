Date Calculator: Calculate Future or Past Date

Need to know what day of the week will be in 45 days? Or what date was 6 months ago? Manually counting days on a calendar, especially taking into account leap years, varying lengths of months and weekends, is inconvenient and prone to errors.

Our Date Calculator is a universal tool that will instantly perform any addition and subtraction operations with dates. It allows you to add or subtract not only calendar days, but also weeks, months, years, and also, most importantly, working days, automatically skipping Saturdays and Sundays.

How to use the calculator?

The calculation takes only a few seconds. We've set today's date as the default, but you can choose any date.

  1. Start date: Specify the date from which to start counting.
  2. Add/Subtract: Enter the number you want to add.
    • For subtraction just enter negative number (For example,-30).
  3. Unit: Select what exactly you think:
    • Days
    • Working days (Mon-Fri)
    • weeks
    • Months
    • Years
  4. Click "Calculate".

The calculator will instantly show not only the final date, but also the day of the week, the number of the day in the year and the total number of calendar days that have passed.

Main advantages: Calculation of working days

The key feature of our instrument is the ability to operate working days. This is indispensable for business and legal tasks.

  • Calendar days: Includes all days in a row (Mon, Tue, Wed, Thu, Fri, Sat, Sun).
  • Working days: Only weekdays are taken into account (Monday - Friday).

Example:

If you add 7 calendar days to Monday, you get the following Monday.

If you add 7 working days to Monday, you will miss 2 days off (Sat, Sun) and get Wednesday of the next week.
