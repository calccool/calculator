Working time standards in the Republic of Kazakhstan

According to the Labor Code of the Republic of Kazakhstan, normal working hours should not exceed 40 hours per week. However, for certain categories of workers, a reduced duration is provided.

Calculation methods and formulas

The calculation is made based on data from the official production calendar of the Republic of Kazakhstan. The main algorithm includes determining the average daily norm and multiplying it by the number of days actually worked.

  1. Definition of average daily rate ( $H_{day}$ ): $$H_{day} = \frac{H_{week}}{D_{week}}$$ Where $H_{week}$ is the established norm per week (for example, 40), and $D_{week}$ is the number of working days per week (5 or 6).
  2. Calculation of the total number of hours ( $T$ ): $$T = (D_{work} — D_{absent}) \times H_{day}$$ Where $D_{work}$ is the number of working days in a period according to the calendar, and $D_{absent}$ is the days the employee is absent.
