How the calculation algorithm works

The system uses multivariate analysis, dividing each event into specific categories. For each category (wedding, work, health) priority vibration numbers are established.

  1. Universal day number: The sum of all digits of the planned date, reduced to a single digit.
  2. Personal number of the initiator: It is calculated based on the date of birth and determines individual compatibility with the energy of the day.
  3. Synergy of partners: When you enable the “Take into account partner” option, the system calculates the arithmetic mean of the vibrations of both participants, which is critically important for wedding celebrations.

To determine the basic vibration, the numerological convolution formula is used:

$$X = \sum_{i=1}^{n} d_i \pmod 9$$

Where $d$ are the date digits, and the result is converted to a number from 1 to 9 (if the remainder is 0, the number is taken to be 9).
