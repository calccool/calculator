To successfully trade on the marketplace, it is not enough just to know the purchase price. This interactive tool allows you to instantly decompose the price of a product and see the real income that will go to your bank account.

Key components of margin

The effectiveness of sales on Yandex Market depends on three basic factors that are integrated into our algorithm:

  • Accommodation costs: The percentage that the marketplace takes for providing the storefront.
  • Logistics chain: The cost of order processing at the warehouse and delivery to the buyer.
  • Financial services: Costs of accepting payments (acquiring) and frequency of payments.

How the calculation algorithm works

Our calculator uses current marketplace formulas. The basic formula for calculating net profit is as follows:

$$Profit = P — (C_{cat} + C_{acq} + C_{log})$$

Where:

  • $P$ — Product selling price.
  • $C_{cat}$ - Commission for placement in the category.
  • $C_{acq}$ — Acquiring costs.
  • $C_{log}$ - Total logistics costs.

Detailed analysis of logistics costs

One of the most difficult elements to calculate manually is the Last Mile. It is calculated as a percentage of the price, but has strict limits, which we took into account in the code:

$$C_{last\_mile} = \max(60, \min(400, P \times 0.055))$$

This means that even for very cheap goods, delivery will not cost less than 60 rubles, and for expensive ones it will not exceed 400 rubles. Additionally, physical weight is taken into account: if your product is heavier than 5 kg, an extra charge for each extra kilogram is added to the processing cost.
