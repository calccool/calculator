Working time standard calculator in Armenia: online calculation according to the RA Labor Code

In the Republic of Armenia, the rules for recording working hours are strictly regulated. The main document is the RA Labor Code (RA Labor Code). For a correct calculation, it is necessary to take into account not only the number of working days in a month, but also shortened days, as well as periods of actual absence of the employee.

Legislative framework and standards

According to current legislation, there are several key parameters that influence the final figure:

  • Maximum duration: no more than 40 hours per week Art. 139 TC RA.
  • Standard day: with a five-day working week, the norm is 8 hours a day.
  • Pre-holidays: The duration of the working day preceding a non-working holiday or memorial day is reduced by 1 hour. 147 TC RA.

Calculation formulas

To calculate accurate indicators, the calculator uses the following mathematical algorithms:

  1. Full time limit for a month: $$T_{full} = (D_{work} \times 8) — D_{pre}$$ where:
    • $T_{full}$ - full monthly hours;
    • $D_{work}$ — number of working days according to the calendar;
    • $8$ - standard shift;
    • $D_{pre}$ — number of pre-holiday days.
  2. Adjusted rate taking into account omissions: $$T_{actual} = T_{full} — (D_{missed} \times 8)$$ where $D_{missed}$ - days of absence, vacation, sick leave, time off.
  3. Cost of one hour: $$R_{hour} = \frac{S}{T_{full}}$$ where $S$ is the established salary before taxes.

Instructions for using the calculator

The tool works in two modes: simple calculation of the norm and advanced calculation with determination of the cost per hour.

Determination of standard hours

  1. Working days: Enter the number of working days in the current month. Usually it is 20, 21 or 22 days.
  2. Pre-holidays: Indicate the number of days on which work is reduced by 1 hour. If there are no such days, leave “0”.
  3. Missed days: If you took a vacation or were sick, indicate the number of working days that fell during this period.

Payroll calculation by hour

Switch the tab to mode "Payroll calculation (hourly)" to see financial analytics:

  • Enter your full salary in AMD drams.
  • The system will automatically calculate how much 1 hour of your work costs at full rate. This is critical for calculating overtime or weekend pay.

Calculation example

Let's say there are 21 working days and 1 pre-holiday day in a month. The employee was sick for 3 working days. The salary is 300,000 AMD.

  • Full norm: $(21 \times 8) — 1 = 167$ hours.
  • Adjusted rate: $167 — (3 \times 8) = 143$ hours.
  • Cost per hour: $300\,000 / 167 \approx 1\,796.41$ AMD.
