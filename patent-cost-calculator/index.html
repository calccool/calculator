Patent cost calculator for foreign citizens

Working in the Russian Federation for citizens of “visa-free” countries requires mandatory registration of a patent. Paying for a patent is not just a state fee, but fixed advance payment for personal income tax. The payment amount changes annually, as it depends on the coefficients established by the state.

This interactive tool allows you to instantly find out the exact amount to be paid depending on the selected region and the validity period of the document, as well as calculate tax savings for officially employed persons.

How is the price of a patent determined?

The value of a patent is not arbitrary. According to the Tax Code of the Russian Federation, the calculation is made using a strict mathematical formula that takes into account inflation processes and regional characteristics of the labor market.

Advance payment calculation formula

The following equation is used to calculate the monthly payment:

$$S = B \times D \times K$$

Where:

  • $S$ — the total cost of the patent for one month;
  • $B$ - the basic value established by law (is 1,200 rubles );
  • $D$ — deflator coefficient, annually approved by the Ministry of Economic Development;
  • $K$ — regional coefficient (set by the authorities of a specific constituent entity of the Russian Federation).

Personal income tax reimbursement through the employer

One of the key functions of our calculator is the section “Reimbursement from personal income tax”. According to Article 227.1 of the Tax Code of the Russian Federation, a foreign citizen has the right to reduce the tax calculated on his salary by the amount of “checks” already paid for a patent.

How does this work:

  1. You pay for the patent (for example, 3 months in advance).
  2. Your employer submits an application to the tax office.
  3. After receiving confirmation, the accounting department stops deducting 13% from your salary until the amount of deductions is equal to the amount of payment for the patent.

Step-by-step instructions for using the calculator

To get the correct data for your case, follow these steps:

Cost calculation

  • Choose yours region of work from the drop-down list. If your city is not in the list, select “Specify manually” and enter the current rate.
  • Specify number of months that you plan to pay. Remember that a patent can be paid for for a period of 1 to 12 months.
  • Click the button "Calculate" to see the total and a visual expiration bar.

Tax deduction calculation

  • Switch to tab “Reimbursement from personal income tax”.
  • Enter your official salary before taxes (salary).
  • The system will automatically show how much of the tax you can return and whether you will still need to pay additional personal income tax to the budget.

Important rules for paying for a patent

Violation of the rules for making payments leads to immediate cancellation of the document. Follow these guidelines:

  • Dates: Pay for the patent at least 3-4 days before the end date of the previous period. Even a delay of 1 day makes the patent invalid.
  • Details: Always check that the Taxpayer Identification Number (TIN) and area code on your receipt are correct.
  • Storage: Be sure to save all receipts (paper or electronic). They will be needed to renew the patent and obtain a tax deduction.
