Square calculator: Online calculation of area, perimeter and diagonal

A square is a fundamental geometric figure, a regular quadrilateral in which all sides are equal and the angles are 90 degrees. Our universal calculator allows you to instantly solve any geometric or construction problem related to a square, performing calculations in forward and reverse order.

Main features of the calculator

This tool is designed not only for school tasks, but also for practical use in construction (calculation of tiles, laminate flooring, wall area). The script supports calculations using any of the known parameters:

  • On the side ( $a$ ): Basic calculation of all properties.
  • Diagonal ( $d$ ): If the size between opposite corners is known.
  • By area ( $S$ ) or perimeter ( $P$ ): Reverse engineering calculation to find side length.

Formulas for calculating square parameters

To understand the logic of calculations, below are the main mathematical relationships used in geometry.

Square area ( $S$ )

Area characterizes the size of the space limited by the sides of the figure.

  • Through side: $$S = a^2$$
  • Across the diagonal: $$S = \frac{d^2}{2}$$
  • Through the perimeter: $$S = \left(\frac{P}{4}\right)^2$$

Square Perimeter ( $P$ )

The perimeter is the total length of the shape's border.

  • Formula: $$P = 4 \times a$$

Square Diagonal ( $d$ )

A line segment connecting opposite vertices. Divides a square into two equal right-angled isosceles triangles.

  • Formula (via Pythagorean theorem): $$d = a \sqrt{2} \approx 1.414 a$$

Inscribed and Circumscribed Circles

In engineering problems and drawing, you often need to find the radii of circles associated with a square.

  1. Inscribed Circle ( $r$ ): Touches the midpoints of all sides of the square. Its diameter is equal to the side of the square. $$r = \frac{a}{2}$$
  2. Circumcircle ( $R$ ): Passes through all four vertices of the square. Its diameter is equal to the diagonal of the square. $$R = \frac{d}{2} = \frac{a\sqrt{2}}{2}$$

Practical Application

Our calculator is equipped with a cost calculation function. This is especially useful when planning renovations.

  • Calculation of floor coverings: Knowing the area of ​​the room (if it is square) and the price per square meter of tiles or laminate, you will immediately get the final budget.
  • Cutting materials: Knowing the diagonal, you can understand whether the sheet material will fit into the opening or how best to position the parts when cutting.

How to use the tool

  1. Select a parameter you know from the drop-down list (side, diagonal, area or perimeter).
  2. Enter a value in the appropriate field.
  3. If necessary, indicate the price per unit of area to calculate the estimate.
  4. Click the button "Calculate" to get a complete geometric analysis of the figure.
