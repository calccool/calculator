Numerological Calculation (The Math Behind)

For a deeper analysis, we use the formula for calculating the “Life Path Number” ( $Life Path Number$ ). This lets you know if you and your friend are looking in the same direction.

The calculation formula looks like this:

$$LPN = \sum (Day) + \sum (Month) + \sum (Year)$$

Where the sum is reduced to a single digit number from 1 to 9.

Example: If you have a path number 3 (creativity), and from a friend 3 or 5 (freedom), the calculator will add bonus points to your friendship power.

Frequently asked questions (FAQ)

Do I need to enter the exact date of birth?

Yes, it is desirable. The date of birth allows the algorithm to determine the zodiac sign and calculate numerological compatibility, which makes the result much more accurate than just guessing by name.

Does the order of names affect the results?

No, rearranging the places of the terms (friends' names) does not change the strength of your friendship.

Is it possible to use diminutive names?

We recommend entering full names (for example, Alexander, not Sasha), as they carry more complete energy information. But for fun, you can check nicknames too!
