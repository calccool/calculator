How the calculation algorithm works

To calculate the position of the nodes, the Mean Node calculation is used based on the Julian date. The mathematical model is based on the position of the ecliptic relative to the J2000.0 epoch. The basic formula for calculating the longitude of the Ascending Node ( $\Omega$ ):

$$\Omega = 125.04452 — 0.0529539 \times d$$

Where:

  • $d$ — the number of days that have passed since the J2000.0 epoch (2451545.0 JD).
  • $\Omega$ (Rahu) — the resulting value in degrees, which is then converted to the $0–360°$ range to determine the Zodiac sign.
  • Ketu - is always in opposition to Rahu (Rahu $+ 180°$ ).

Two poles of your destiny

The lunar nodes always work in pairs, creating the so-called “karmic axis”. Understanding this axis helps you find a balance between what you already know and what you have yet to learn.

South Node (Ketu) - Fulcrum

Ketu symbolizes your “baggage” from past lives or the genetic memory of your family. These are talents that manifest themselves, and situations in which everything is familiar to you. However, excessive immersion in the energy of Ketu leads to degradation, since there is no development here, only the exploitation of an old resource.

North Node (Rahu) - Development Vector

Rahu points to a sphere that seems frightening and incomprehensible, but it is in it that your maximum growth is hidden. This is your purpose in your current incarnation. Realization according to Rahu is often accompanied by social success if a person overcomes internal resistance.

Karmic exams: when fate tests us

The nodes make a complete revolution around the zodiac circle in approximately 18.6 years. These periods in astrology are called “return of the nodes” - the time when fate checks whether you are following the path of your destiny.

Key screening ages:

  • 18–19 years old: The first serious self-determination. Choosing a life path.
  • 27–28 years old: Half return of knots. A crisis of reference points, the first test of “adulthood.”
  • 37–38 years old: Second return. The most important period when life can change dramatically (change of profession, move) if you ignored the tasks of Rahu.
  • 56–57 years old: Third return. Time to sum up and share experience.

If a person lives in harmony with his North Node, return periods bring gifts of fate and new opportunities. If he is “stuck” in the South Node, difficult life trials are possible, forcing him to forcibly change course.
