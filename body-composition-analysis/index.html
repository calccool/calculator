Formulas and scientific basis

We use the “Gold Standard” formulas in nutrition and sports medicine. Here's how it works under the hood:

1. Basal metabolic rate (RMR/BMR)

To calculate the energy required by the body at rest, we use Mifflin-San Geor formula. The American Dietetic Association recognizes it as the most accurate to date.

$$P = (10 \times m) + (6.25 \times h) — (5 \times a) + s$$

Where:

  • $m$ - body weight (kg)
  • $h$ - height (cm)
  • $a$ - age (years)
  • $s$ - gender coefficient (+5 for men, -161 for women)

2. Calculation of body fat percentage (US Navy Method)

This method has a correlation of up to 97% with professional hydrostatic weighing.

For men, the formula looks like this:

$$\%Fat = 495 / (1.0324 — 0.19077 \times \log_{10}(waist — neck) + 0.15456 \times \log_{10}(height)) — 450$$

3. Target heart rate (Karvonen Method)

The most accurate way to determine your heart rate for cardio because it takes into account your resting heart rate (RHR).

$$THR = ((MaxHR — RHR) \times k) + RHR$$

Where $k$ is the intensity factor (for example, 0.6–0.8 for fat burning).

How to take measurements correctly?

To obtain a reliable result (<1% error), follow these rules for measuring with a centimeter tape:

  • Neck: Measure under your Adam's apple with your muscles relaxed. The tape should not choke.
  • Waist:
    • Men: At the level of the navel.
    • Women: At the narrowest point (usually just above the navel). Don't suck in your stomach!
  • Hips (women only): In the widest part of the buttocks.
  • Elbow width: Bend your arm at a 90° angle with your fingers pointing up. Measure the distance between the bones of the elbow joint (it is better to use a caliper or ruler).

Decoding the results

What does your body type mean?

  • 🍎Apple: Fat accumulates in the abdominal area (visceral fat). This increases the risk of cardiovascular disease. A low-carb diet and cardio are recommended.
  • 🍐 Pear: Fat is deposited on the thighs and buttocks. This is less dangerous to health, but more difficult to correct. Strength training your legs will help balance your proportions.
  • ⌛ Hourglass: Perfect balance of proportions.

Recommendations for BZHU

The calculator automatically suggests nutrient balances.

  • Proteins: Construction material. Critical when losing weight to avoid losing muscle.
  • Fats: Necessary for the hormonal system. Do not lower them below 0.8 g per kg of weight.
  • Carbohydrates: The main source of energy. Manipulate them depending on your activity level.
