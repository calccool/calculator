Formulas for calculating biological age

The calculator uses precise mathematical models to determine your progress. You can calculate these values ​​yourself using the following formulas:

1. Remaining days in the current stage:

$$D_{left} = L_{max} — D_{lived}$$

Where:

  • $D_{left}$ - number of days until birthday;
  • $L_{max}$ — total duration of the current stage for the selected mode;
  • $D_{lived}$ - the number of days the Sim has already lived (displayed in the age panel in the game).

2. Total life path (from birth):

$$D_{total} = \sum_{i=1}^{n-1} S_i + D_{lived}$$

Where:

  • $\sum S_i$ - the sum of days of all previous completed stages;
  • $n$ — serial number of the current stage.
