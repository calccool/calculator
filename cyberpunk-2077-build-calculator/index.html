Cyberpunk 2077 characteristics and builds calculator

With the release of Update 2.0 and the Phantom Liberty expansion, the skill and stat system in Cyberpunk 2077 has undergone fundamental changes. Our interactive calculator will help you distribute your stat points so that you can achieve maximum effectiveness in battle without wasting a single point.

How the build calculator works

The tool takes into account all basic game limits. When creating a character, you start with 3 points in each category and have a pool of free points. As you level up to level 60 (the maximum level with the DLC installed), the total number of available stat points reaches 81.

The math of your survival in Night City

Your build is not just numbers, but real bonuses to survivability and damage. The calculator automatically calculates secondary indicators based on the formulas included in the game:

  • Health: Each point of Strength increases your HP.
  • Critical Chance: The reaction directly affects the chance of landing a critical hit.
  • Implant capacity: Technology determines how much powerful cybersoftware you can install on yourself.
  • Critical Damage: Coolness makes your sneak attacks and headshots deadly.

Bonus calculation formulas

For those who want to know exactly how boosts are calculated, we'll use the official Patch 2.1 odds:

$$Bonus_{HP} = (Body — 3) \times 2$$

$$Bonus_{CritChance} = (Reflexes — 3) \times 0.5\%$$

$$Bonus_{Cyberware} = (Tech — 3) \times 2$$

$$Bonus_{RAM} = \lfloor (Int — 3) / 4 \rfloor \times 1$$

$$Bonus_{CritDmg} = (Cool — 3) \times 1.25\%$$

Detailed analysis of characteristics

Strength (Body)

Determines physical strength and endurance. Required for builds through shotguns, machine guns and fists (gorilla hands). Increases maximum health.

Reflexes

Affects maneuverability and speed. If you use katanas, assault rifles or submachine guns, this is your priority stat. Gives a bonus to critical hit chance.

Technical Ability

The most important feature in version 2.0+. She is responsible for the “Implant Limit”, allowing you to install more legendary software, and strengthens electromagnetic weapons.

Intelligence

A key attribute for netrunners. Increases cyberdeck memory capacity (RAM) and script efficiency. Every 4 points above the base gives +1 to the amount of RAM.

Cool

Affects accuracy, stealth and critical damage. Ideal for snipers, knife assassins and pistol/revolver users.

Tips for creating a build

  1. Don't strive for universality: The points are enough to develop three characteristics to the maximum (20) and leave a little more for the fourth.
  2. Consider the level: If the calculator shows that you need level 60, then the build is intended for endgame and requires Phantom Liberty.
  3. Follow the visualization: Our “Development Profile” graph clearly shows the distortions of your build. A balanced character is good, but a highly specialized Night City Legend is always more effective.
