Transport tax calculator: online calculation by regions of the Russian Federation and capacity

Transport tax is a regional fee that every owner of a registered vehicle is required to pay. The payment amount directly depends on the engine power, the type of vehicle and the federal subject in which the owner is registered. Our online calculator allows you to instantly calculate the exact amount, eliminating errors in manual calculations.

Tax calculation formula

To calculate the final amount, the tax office uses a standard mathematical model. The final value is formed using the following formula:

$$T = P \cdot R \cdot \frac{m}{12} \cdot K$$

Where the variables mean the following:

  • $T$ — The total amount of tax payable (in rubles).
  • $P$ — Engine power in horsepower (hp).
  • $R$ — Tax rate established in a specific region for a given power category.
  • $m$ — The number of complete months of car ownership in the reporting year.
  • $K$ — Increasing coefficient (the so-called “luxury tax”).

Increasing factor on luxury cars

If the value of your car exceeds the established threshold, an increasing factor is applied in the calculation. Since 2022, the rules have been simplified, and now the coefficient of 3.0 applies only to cars from the list of the Ministry of Industry and Trade, the average cost of which is from 10 million rubles.

The main nuances of using the coefficient

  • List of the Ministry of Industry and Trade: The list of models is updated annually until March 31.
  • Car age: For some models, the coefficient ceases to apply when the car reaches a certain age (for example, 10 or 20 years).
  • Single multiplier: Currently, a single coefficient $3.0$ is used for all cars from the “luxury” list.

Ownership months: 15th rule

When calculating tax for an incomplete year (in the case of buying or selling a vehicle), it is extremely important to take into account the “full month” rule:

  1. If the car is registered until the 15th inclusive, the month is considered full (the owner pays for it).
  2. If deregistration occurs after the 15th, this month is also included in the seller's tax calculation.
  3. Otherwise, the month in the billing period of this owner is not taken into account.
