Accurate age calculator - calculate years, months, days, hours and seconds

Age calculator is a simple tool that helps you determine exactly how long you have lived from your birthday to the current moment. It takes into account leap years, differences in months and days, and shows not only full years, but also the exact number of months, weeks, days, hours, minutes and seconds.

What does the age calculator show?

  • Full age: years, months and days.
  • Lived time in days, weeks, hours and minutes.
  • Number of days until next birthday.
  • Next birthday date.
  • The difference between any two dates (for example, how much time has passed since an important event).
  • Age in “alternative units”: weeks, months and days.

How to use the age calculator

  1. Enter your date of birth.
  2. Check that the current date is entered automatically (or change manually).
  3. Click "Calculate".
  4. Look at the full data - years, months, days and lifetime.
  5. If desired, enter a second date (for example, wedding, enrollment, move) to find out the difference.

Why is accurate calculation important?

  • For visa and pension documents where exact age is required to the day.
  • In medical and sports calculations (heart rate, dosage, fitness programs).
  • In educational systems where it is important to calculate the child’s age on a certain date.
  • For statistics, genealogy, personal planning and just curiosity.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I find out how old I will be in 10 years?

Enter your date of birth and set the current date to the desired future date → get a calculation.

Does leap year count?

Yes - the formulas are calculated based on the UTC date and take the calendar into account correctly.

Is it possible to compare with another date?

Yes - the Additional Date field allows you to find out how much time has passed or remains until the specified date.
