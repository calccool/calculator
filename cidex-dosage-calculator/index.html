Sidex dosage calculator for an aquarium: calculation by volume and size

Sidex (a drug based on 2.5% glutaraldehyde ) is one of the most effective means to combat unwanted algae in an aquarium. However, its use requires pinpoint precision: an insufficient dose will be useless, and an excess dose will be dangerous for the inhabitants.

This online calculator is designed to automate calculations, eliminating the risk of human error. It takes into account not only the total displacement, but also the displacement of water by soil, as well as the specifics of your problem.

Mathematical formula for calculation

The daily dose ( $D$ ) in milliliters is calculated using the formula:

$$D = \frac{V_{net} \cdot R}{100}$$

Where:

  • $V_{net}$ - net volume of water in liters (minus decorations and soil).
  • $R$ - selected intensity factor (from 15 to 30).

If you do not know the exact volume of water, the calculator will calculate it based on the size of the glass, applying a correction for underfilling and substrate:

$$V_{net} = \frac{L \cdot W \cdot H}{1000} \cdot 0.85$$

(where 0.85 is the useful volume coefficient, taking into account 15% for decor).

Instructions for safe use

To ensure that the use of Sidex brings benefits and not harm, adhere to strict regulations:

The golden rules of the aquarist

  • Daylight hours: Apply the drug in the morning, immediately after turning on the lights. At this time, plants begin to actively consume carbon, and the risk of hypoxia for fish is minimal.
  • Aeration is life: Glutaraldehyde actively binds oxygen during oxidation. Be sure to turn on the compressor at maximum for the entire course period.
  • Course treatment: To achieve a stable result, the drug is applied daily for 7–14 days. A single application only temporarily inhibits algae.
  • Final stage: After completing the course of treatment, it is recommended to change 30–50% of the water to remove the decay products of dead organic matter.

How to use the calculator

  1. Select mode: “Volume is known” (if you know the net displacement) or “By size” (if you need to calculate the volume from scratch).
  2. Specify parameters: Enter the length, width and actual height of the water column (not the height of the aquarium itself!).
  3. Select a target: From mild prevention to radical fight against “beard”.
  4. Get the diagram: The system will give the daily dose in milliliters and “caps” (standard 5 ml), and also calculate the total volume of the drug for the entire course.

Compatibility and Risks

Sidex in therapeutic doses (up to 20 ml/100 l) is safe for most fish and plants. However, species such as valisneria, hornwort and some mosses, may become sensitive and “dissolve” during a long course. If you notice lethargic behavior of fish (swallowing air at the surface), immediately change the water and increase aeration.
