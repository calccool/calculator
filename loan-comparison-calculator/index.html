Mathematical calculation model

To ensure banking accuracy, we use an annuity payment formula that is used in 99% of consumer loans and mortgages.

Formula for calculating monthly payment:

PMT = S * (r * (1 + r)^n) / ((1 + r)^n - 1)

Where:

  • PMT — monthly payment (monthly payment).
  • S — loan amount (loan amount).
  • r — monthly interest rate (monthly rate: annual / 12 / 100).
  • n - number of months (period in months).

After calculating the base payment, our algorithm adds monthly fees to it and adds one-time fees to the total cost of the loan, creating Honest full cost of the loan.
