Mathematical formula for calculation

To determine compatibility, we use the method of sequentially adding the digits of the date of birth to obtain a prime number (from 1 to 9). This process is called "numerological convolution".

Formula for calculating an individual code:

If the date of birth is $D_1.M_1.Y_1$ , then the life path number $X$ is calculated as:

$$X = \sum (digits \ of \ D_1, M_1, Y_1) \pmod 9$$

Example:

For date $15.06.1992$ :

$1 + 5 + 0 + 6 + 1 + 9 + 9 + 2 = 33 \rightarrow 3 + 3 = 6$.
