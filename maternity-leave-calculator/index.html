Maternity payments calculator: online calculation of benefits for BiR

Maternity benefit (M&B) is a one-time payment that an officially employed woman receives when she goes on maternity leave. In 2026, the calculation system is based on income data for the two previous calendar years (2024 and 2025).

Our maternity payments calculator allows you to instantly find out the final amount, taking into account all the nuances of the legislation: from part-time work to regional coefficients and maximum limits of the Social Fund of Russia (SFR).

Algorithm and calculation formula

The average daily earnings are used to calculate the amount. The basic formula looks like this:

$$P = \frac{D_1 + D_2}{730 — D_{ex}} \times T$$

Where:

  • $P$ — total benefit amount;
  • $D_1, D_2$ — official income for the first and second accounting years;
  • $730$ - the number of calendar days in a two-year period (can be 731 or 732 if leap years);
  • $D_{ex}$ — excluded days (sick leave, periods of last maternity leave);
  • $T$ — total duration of maternity leave in days.

Basic parameters and limits

The size of the payment directly depends on the borders established by the state. The benefit cannot be lower than the amount calculated according to the minimum wage and higher than the maximum limit established by the SFR.

Duration of maternity leave

The period for which sick leave is issued depends on the course of pregnancy and the number of children:

  • 140 days: Standard situation (70 days before birth and 70 after).
  • 156 days: Complicated childbirth (for example, caesarean section).
  • 194 days: Multiple pregnancy (twins, triplets or more).

Important: The benefit is paid in total for the entire vacation period immediately after the sick leave is provided to the employer. Personal income tax (NDFL) is not withheld from this amount.

Excluded days and replacement of years

If in the billing period (2024–2025) you were already on leave for labor or child care, these years can be replace to earlier dates (for example, 2022 or 2023) if this would result in an increase in the benefit amount.

Also subtracted from the total number of days (730):

  1. Periods of temporary incapacity for work (sick leave).
  2. Periods of release from work with retention of salary, if no contributions to the SFR were accrued for it.

Using our advanced mode will allow you to specify these days so as not to artificially lower your average earnings.

Step-by-step instructions for using the calculator

  1. Enter income: Enter the total gross amount (before taxes) for two full years.
  2. Select type of maternity leave: Set the number of days according to your sick leave certificate.
  3. Excluded days: If you were sick, indicate the number of days in the appropriate field (relevant for the “Advanced mode”).
  4. Specify the parameters: If you work in the Far North or on a part-time basis, set up a regional coefficient and rate share.
  5. Get the result: The system will provide detailed information about the payment, including a schedule for the distribution of funds before and after childbirth.
