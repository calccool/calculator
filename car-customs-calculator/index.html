What does the customs payment consist of?

The final cost of legalizing a car in the Russian Federation consists of four main components:

  1. Customs clearance fee — a fixed amount depending on the cost of the car.
  2. Customs duty (Flat rate) - the main tax, the amount of which depends on the age of the car, engine type and its volume.
  3. Recycling fee — payment for future disposal of the vehicle. From 2024–2025, rates are significantly differentiated for personal and commercial use.
  4. VAT and Excise — used primarily for legal entities and electric vehicles.

Calculation formulas for individuals

For citizens importing a car for personal use (no more than 1 car per year), the Unified Customs Rate (UTR). It depends on the age of the car and engine displacement.

Cars aged from 3 to 5 years

This is the most profitable category for import. The calculation is made based on engine volume ( $V$ in cm³):

  • If $V \le 1000$ cm³: $$Duty = V \times 1.5€$$
  • If $1000 < V \le 1500$ cm³: $$Duty = V \times 1.7€$$
  • If $1500 < V \le 1800$ cm³: $$Duty = V \times 2.5€$$
  • If $1800 < V \le 2300$ cm³: $$Duty = V \times 2.7€$$
  • If $2300 < V \le 3000$ cm³: $$Duty = V \times 3.0€$$
  • If $V > 3000$ cm³: $$Duty = V \times 3.6€$$

New cars (up to 3 years)

Here the calculation is more complicated: either a percentage of the cost or a volume rate is taken (whichever is greater is chosen).

$$Duty = \max(Price \times 48\%, V \times Rate_{euro})$$

Features of the recycling fee in 2026

The recycling fee has become the main instrument for regulating the market. It is important to understand the difference between preferential and commercial ratios.

Preferential coefficient applies if:

  • The car is imported by an individual.
  • Engine size does not exceed 3.0 liters.
  • The car remains the payer's property for at least 12 months.

Commercial coefficient applies if:

  • The car is imported by a legal entity.
  • The second car is being imported in a year.
  • Engine capacity exceeds 3.0 liters (even for individuals!).
  • The car is sold earlier than a year after import.

Additional import costs

Our calculator allows you to add to the calculation the inevitable associated expenses that sellers often keep silent about:

  1. SBCTS and EPTS: Without issuing a Design Safety Certificate and an Electronic PTS, you will not be able to register your car with the traffic police. The average cost of a set of laboratory services and certification is about 60,000 rubles.
  2. ERA-GLONASS: Mandatory installation of an emergency call button. The cost of equipment with installation varies between 30,000 - 50,000 rubles.
  3. Broker services: A specialist who will correctly fill out the declaration and transport the car through the customs warehouse (SWH).
