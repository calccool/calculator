Online date of death calculator - find out how long you have left to live

The Date of Death Calculator is a unique online tool that estimates your life expectancy and estimated date of death based on statistics and individual factors. The algorithm takes into account gender, age, lifestyle, physical activity level, habits and overall health to provide the most accurate prediction possible in an entertaining yet realistic manner.

How does the life expectancy calculator work?

The calculation is based on real data from the World Health Organization on average life expectancy by continent and gender. Then adjustments are made: excess weight, lack of sleep, stress, smoking, alcohol and low physical activity reduce the expected duration, and proper sleep, healthy eating and exercise increase it.
As a result, the program shows an approximate date of death, remaining number of years, total life expectancy, body mass index (BMI) and even biological age assessment.

What data is needed for calculation

  • Gender and date of birth
  • Height and weight (to calculate BMI)
  • Continent of residence
  • Smoking and drinking alcohol
  • Physical activity and sleep duration
  • Stress level and presence of hypertension

These parameters allow you to simulate a real statistical trend and assess how much your lifestyle is bringing you closer or further to old age and the risks of premature death.

Why is this calculator more accurate than its analogues?

  • Uses continental data rather than global averages
  • Takes into account habits, activity, sleep and stress
  • Shows not only the date, but also the seconds remaining until the end of life
  • Gives an estimate of biological age - how much younger or older you are than your passport
  • Works completely locally, without data transfer

Example of result interpretation

If the calculator shows that you will live until 2085, and there are 22,000 days left until the end of your life, this is not a death sentence, but an incentive to think about your habits. Improve your sleep, reduce stress and increase physical activity - and your “date of death” will move forward by years.

It's important to understand

The results are for informational purposes only and do not constitute a medical diagnosis. The algorithm is based on probabilistic models and statistics, not on personal medical data. The calculator was created to help users be conscious of their health and prolong their lives.
