Profit calculation formulas

Our algorithm uses mathematically proven models to determine your final payout.

1. Calculation of net income (Net Profit):

$$R_{net} = R_{gross} \times \frac{P_{share}}{100}$$

Where:

  • $R_{net}$ - Robux that will be added to your balance;
  • $R_{gross}$ - the price you set for the item;
  • $P_{share}$ - percentage of developer income (70 or 30).

2. Conversion to dollars (USD):

$$Cash_{usd} = R_{total} \times 0.0035$$

The DevEx system rate is fixed at $0.0035 for 1 Robux (or $105 за каждые 30 000 R$ ).
