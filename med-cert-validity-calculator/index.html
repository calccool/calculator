Calculator for the validity period of medical certificates (traffic police, for weapons, 086/u)

A medical report has a limited “life” period during which it can be presented to government authorities. It is important to distinguish deadline for presentation certificates and validity period of the right itself (for example, owning a weapon or driving a car), which is confirmed by this certificate.

Our calculator takes into account current legislation and allows you to instantly calculate whether a document has expired. Just select the type of certificate and the date it was issued.

Mathematical calculation model

The calculator algorithm makes calculations based on calendar months. The expiration date of $D_{exp}$ is determined by the formula:

$$D_{exp} = D_{start} + M$$

Where:

  • $D_{start}$ — document issue date;
  • $M$ - the number of full months established by law.

The remaining term in days ( $T_{rem}$ ) is calculated as the difference between the end date and the current date (or the selected check date):

$$T_{rem} = \frac{T_{exp} — T_{check}}{86\,400\,000}$$

The result is rounded up to the nearest whole number of days.

Why is it important to check the certificate in advance?

  1. Refusal to accept documents: If at the time of applying to the traffic police the certificate is 1 year and 1 day old, it is considered invalid.
  2. Registration with departments: The queue for submitting documents can be 1-2 weeks. The calculator allows you to check the status of a certificate for a specific date in the future.
  3. Visual control: Our visualization system will show the percentage of document “wear” so that you can schedule a visit to the doctor on time.

Instructions for use

  • Select date: Indicate the day the doctor signed the certificate (usually listed in the upper left corner or printed).
  • Select type: Use the preset values ​​or enter your own duration in months.
  • Check point: If you are planning a visit to the department in a week, turn on “Check for a future date” to make sure that the document will not “burn” by this time.

Expert advice: To obtain a driver's license or weapons license, we recommend that you have at least 14 calendar days before the expiration of the certificate in case of technical failures in the Uniform State Health Information System database.
