Warframe EHP Calculator: Calculate effective frame health

In Warframe, a character's survivability isn't limited to numbers in the corner of the screen. EHP (Effective Health Pool) - This is the total amount of “pure” damage that the enemy must deal to completely reset your health. Understanding this value is critical for high-level content such as Steel Path or Archimedia.

Your actual HP depends on a complex interaction of three factors: your base pool of points (Health and Shields), your armor rating, and global Damage Reduction multipliers.

How does armor affect survivability?

Armor in Warframe reduces damage taken only by Health. It does not protect shields (except for specific mechanics). The higher the armor rating, the more “effective” health points the frame receives for each unit of nominal HP.

To calculate the effectiveness of armor, the following formula is used:

$$Reduction = \frac{Armor}{Armor + 300}$$

This means that every 300 armor units adds an amount of effective health to your frame equal to its nominal reserve. For example, at 300 armor, health damage is reduced by 50%, effectively doubling your HP.

Basic calculation formulas in the calculator

Our tool uses current game formulas to calculate the final survivability. The calculation is divided into two main streams: effective shields and effective health.

Effective Health Formula (EHP Health)

$$EHP_{health} = HP \times (1 + \frac{Armor}{300}) \times \frac{1}{1 — DR}$$

Where DR - this is the sum of all external resists (for example, 90% from the “Adaptation” mod or 95% from Mesa’s “Fragment Shield” ability).

Features of shield mechanics

With the update to the shield system, all Warframes received built-in 50% damage resistance on the shields. This automatically doubles their effectiveness against most types of attacks.

  • Normal mode: The effectiveness of shields is multiplied by 2.
  • Archwing/Necramech Mode: There is no built-in 50% resistance.
  • Toxin Damage: Completely ignores shields, dealing damage directly to health.

Why is it important to consider the type of damage?

Our calculator allows you to switch to the mode "Toxin Damage". This is a critical feature for preparing for missions against the Infected Faction or certain bosses. In this mode, shields are reduced to zero as the toxin passes through them, and your survivability depends solely on the Health + Armor combo.

How to use the EHP calculator

To get the most accurate results, follow these instructions:

  1. Enter basic values: Enter the Health, Armor and Shields from your frame's stats window in the Arsenal.
  2. Add resists: If you are using the Adaptation mod, enter 90%. If your frame has defensive abilities (Gara, Mesa, Nezha), enter the appropriate damage reduction percentage.
  3. Select mode: Use the Toxin Damage switch to see your vulnerability to shield bypass.
  4. Consider the technique: If you are planning a build for a Necramech or Archwing, activate the corresponding checkbox to disable the bonus shield resistance.
