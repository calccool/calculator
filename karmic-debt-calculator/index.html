If the calculator highlights one of the four numbers, this means that you will undergo a certain transformation in your current life.

Number 13: The Duty of Hard Work

Arises from laziness and abuse of other people's labor in the past.

  • Manifestation: The constant feeling that you have to work three times harder than everyone else to achieve success.
  • How to work it out: Discipline, refusal to look for “easy ways” and the habit of bringing every task started to the end.

Number 14: Debt of Abuse of Freedom

Associated with violating the rights of other people or indulging one’s addictions.

  • Manifestation: Chaos in life, frequent changes, difficulties with commitments.
  • How to work it out: Moderation, setting high goals and the ability to find inner stability in a changing world.

Number 16: Debt of a Shattered Ego

The deepest number indicating pride and self-centeredness in past lives.

Number 19: Debt of Abuse of Power

Indicates that in the past the person has used his power to suppress others.

  • Manifestation: Loneliness “at the top”, reluctance to listen to advice, difficulties in getting help.
  • How to work it out: The ability to cooperate, recognition of the value of other people’s opinions and selfless help to loved ones.
