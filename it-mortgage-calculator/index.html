How payout calculation works

The calculator is based on the annuity payment formula. This is a method in which you pay an equal amount each month consisting of a portion of the principal and accrued interest.

The mathematical calculation model is as follows:

$$A = P \times \frac{r(1 + r)^n}{(1 + r)^n — 1}$$

Where variables mean:

  • $A$ - monthly annuity payment;
  • $P$ — loan amount (cost of housing minus down payment);
  • $r$ - monthly interest rate (annual rate / 100 / 12);
  • $n$ - total number of months of lending.

Key program conditions

To successfully pass the bank's verification, you must meet three main groups of requirements: company parameters, borrower parameters and real estate parameters.

Main restrictions in 2026:

  • Interest rate: up to 6% per annum (may be lower if subsidized by a bank or developer).
  • Maximum amount: 9,000,000 rubles for all regions.
  • Down payment: not less than 20% of the contract price.
  • Program duration: until the end of 2030.

Specialist income requirements

The minimum wage level depends on the place of registration of the employing company specified in the employment contract. Please note: it is not the address of your office or place of residence that is important, but the legal address of the organization.

Important nuances for IT specialists

Before using the calculator and placing a trade, there are a few expert recommendations that are often overlooked:

  1. Accreditation and benefits: The company must not only have accreditation from the Ministry of Digital Development, but also actually apply tax benefits. This is a critical condition for maintaining the bet.
  2. Dismissal and risk: If you are fired, you have 6 months to find employment with another accredited IT company. If this is not done, the bank has the right to raise the rate to the market level (Central Bank key rate + fixed interest rate).
  3. Age limit: The program is available to specialists aged 18 to 50 years inclusive at the time of conclusion of the contract.
  4. Tax deduction: Don't forget about the possibility of returning some of the funds. In total, you can receive up to 650,000 rubles (260 thousand for the purchase of housing and 390 thousand for paid interest on the mortgage).
