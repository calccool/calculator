List of public holidays in the Republic of Belarus

The calculator already includes all official non-working holidays established by Presidential Decree No. 157. If your vacation falls on these dates, they are automatically excluded from the calculation of calendar vacation days.

Calculation method and formula

Calculation of the vacation end date is based on a simple but strict mathematical rule. The number of calendar days of rest increases by the number of holidays that fall during this period.

Mathematically, this can be expressed by the following formula:

$$D_{return} = D_{start} + (D_{duration} + D_{holidays})$$

Where:

  • $D_{return}$ - first working day after rest;
  • $D_{start}$ - start date of labor leave;
  • $D_{duration}$ - number of days of leave by order;
  • $D_{holidays}$ — the number of public holidays that fall within the interval.

Important legal nuances

It is important to understand that the holidays for which your vacation is extended are not subject to payment. They only shift the date of your return to work, preserving your right to full paid rest in the number of days stipulated by the contract.

Answers to frequently asked questions

Is the vacation extended if the holiday falls on Saturday or Sunday?

Yes, in Belarus, according to Article 151 of the Labor Code of the Republic of Belarus, it does not matter whether a holiday coincides with a scheduled weekend. In any case, the holiday is not included in the number of vacation days.

How to calculate the amount of vacation pay?

The amount depends on your average earnings over the last 12 months. Enter your average daily earnings into the calculator to get your estimated payout amount.
