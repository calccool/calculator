The mathematics of translation: how it works

The converter is based on a reference unit - cubic meter (m³). Each measure in our database is assigned its own unique coefficient relative to a cubic meter.

When choosing any two units, the system uses a universal mathematical proportion:

$$V_{\text{результат}} = V_{\text{исходный}} \times \frac{K_{\text{исходный}}}{K_{\text{целевой}}}$$

Where:

  • $V$ - volume value.
  • $K$ is the ratio of a specific unit to a cubic meter.

For example, to convert 5 US gallons to liters, the system takes the gallon factor ( $0.00378541$ ) and divides it by the liter factor ( $0.001$ ), then multiplies it by your value, producing a mathematically perfect result.
