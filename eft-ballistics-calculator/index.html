Ballistics and armor penetration calculator in Escape from Tarkov (EFT)

In the world of Tarkov, the outcome of each shootout depends not only on your accuracy, but also on a deep understanding of the mechanics of interaction between bullets and armor. Each hit is a complex mathematical calculation that takes into account the penetration of the cartridge ( Penetration ) and the current state of the body armor.

Using our calculator allows players to evaluate in advance the effectiveness of the selected ammunition against a specific defense class, which is critical for survival in raids.

Key calculation parameters

To accurately predict the result, three basic indicators are taken into account:

  • Penetration ability (Pen): A quantitative indicator of a bullet’s ability to overcome an obstacle.
  • Damage to body: The number of health units (HP) that the target loses when penetrated through or armored.
  • Armor Class: Equipment protection level (from 2 to 6 classes according to the EFT classification).

Mathematical model of penetration

The logic of the calculator is based on calculating the difference between the penetration of the cartridge and the nominal protection value. The basic formula for the probability of penetration is as follows:

$$\Delta = P_{pen} — (AC \cdot 10)$$

$$Chance = \frac{\Delta + 15}{15} \cdot 100\%$$

Where:

  • $P_{pen}$ - cartridge penetration indicator.
  • $AC$ - armor class.
  • $\Delta$ - potential difference (delta).

If the penetration significantly exceeds the protection threshold, the probability approaches 100%. If the bullet does not penetrate the body armor, the target receives the so-called armor damage (blunt damage), which is approximately 8% of the base damage of the cartridge.

Armor classes and their features in battle

The choice of body armor directly affects your mobility and survivability. The calculator presents all current protection classes:

Light protection (2–3 class)

Provides protection against pistol bullets and shot. Virtually useless against assault rifles using intermediate cartridges.

Middle segment (4th grade)

"Gold Standard" by Tarkov. Protects against most budget cartridges, such as 5.45 PS or 7.62 PS, but quickly loses strength when fired by armor-piercing ammunition.

Heavy equipment (grade 5–6)

Elite body armor and plates (Slick, Zhuk-6a). They are capable of withstanding several hits from even top-end cartridges, but they have a high cost and significant penalties for ergonomics.

How to use the calculator

  1. Enter cartridge parameters: Enter the Penetration and Damage values ​​(they can be found in the current ballistics tables).
  2. Use presets: For quick calculations, we have added buttons for popular ammunition (7.62 BP, M61, etc.).
  3. Select armor class: Specify your opponent's defense class to see a visualization of the chances.
  4. Analyze graphs: The calculator will automatically build a probability chart and a scale of expected damage based on HP and armor strength.

Important: Remember that each hit destroys the armor, reducing its effective class. Even a weak cartridge after 10-15 hits can destroy the Slick and begin to cause full damage to the body.
