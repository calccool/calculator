Yandex.Direct budget calculator: Forecast of clicks, leads and profit by CPC

Effective launch of contextual advertising is impossible without preliminary media planning. Using a calculator allows you to avoid wasting your budget by calculating critical payback indicators in advance. Unlike standard tools, this calculator supports two scenarios: planning from available funds and calculating the required budget to achieve specific sales goals.

The tool takes into account not only the cost per click (CPC), but also website conversion, as well as financial indicators of your business, such as average bill and margin.

Main features of the calculator

  • Flexible forecasting: calculation “from the budget” or “from the sales plan”.
  • Funnel visualization: a visual graph of how impressions turn into real money.
  • Financial analysis: automatic calculation of net profit after deducting all expenses.
  • Payback analysis: instant determination of ROMI (return on investment).
  • Interactive interface: visual cues and the ability to instantly copy results.

How to calculate an advertising budget: Formulas and logic

The calculator is based on classic internet marketing formulas. Understanding these dependencies helps you better manage your advertising campaigns.

Key calculation formulas

To obtain accurate data, the calculator uses the following mathematical models:

  1. Number of clicks: $$Clicks = \frac{Budget}{CPC}$$
  2. Impression forecast (for a given CTR): $$Impressions = \frac{Clicks}{CTR \cdot 0.01}$$
  3. Cost per lead (CPL): $$CPL = \frac{Budget}{Leads}$$
  4. Calculation of ROMI (return on marketing): $$ROMI = \frac{Net Profit}{Marketing Spend} \cdot 100\%$$

2 planning modes: From budget and from goal

The calculator adapts to your business tasks, offering two fundamentally different approaches to planning.

Scenario 1: “I have a fixed budget”

In this mode, you enter the amount you are willing to spend per month. The calculator will show how many clicks to the site you will receive at the current click price, how much one application will cost and whether this volume is enough to become a profit.

Scenario 2: “I need X number of sales per month”

The “From Plan to Budget” mode is indispensable for sales departments. You indicate the desired number of transactions, and the algorithm calculates what advertising budget needs to be protected in front of management, taking into account the current funnel conversion rates.

How to interpret results (ROMI and funnel)

After pressing the button "Calculate", the system builds a visual traffic funnel. This allows you to see bottlenecks in marketing. For example, if you see a tiny number of sales despite a huge number of clicks, the problem may be low website conversion (CR1) or poor sales department performance (CR2).

Particular attention should be paid to the indicator ROMI:

  • ROMI > 0%: Advertising pays off, your work is a plus.
  • ROMI = 0%: Break-even point (you are working “to zero”).
  • ROMI < 0%: The advertising campaign is unprofitable; optimization of settings or sales funnel is required.
