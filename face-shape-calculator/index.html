Face Shape Calculator: Online Measurement Test

How to find out your face shape? Exact method

Many people try to determine the shape of their face “by eye,” tracing the reflection in the mirror with lipstick, but this method often produces errors. Our online calculator uses a mathematical approach based on the relationship between four key parameters of your face.

Knowing your shape (oval, round, square, heart-shaped, etc.), you can accurately select haircuts, glasses shape, beard style and makeup (contouring) techniques.

Instructions: How to take measurements?

To get an accurate result, you will need a flexible measuring tape (sewing tape measure). Stand in front of a mirror in good lighting:

  1. Forehead width (F): Measure the distance between the widest points of your forehead (usually halfway between your eyebrows and your hairline).
  2. Cheek width (C): Find the most prominent points of your cheekbones under the outer corners of your eyes and measure the distance between them.
  3. Jaw Width (J): Measure from the corner of your jaw (under your ear) to the tip of your chin and multiply by 2 (or measure from ear to ear along your jawline).
  4. Face Length(L): Measure from the center point of your hairline to the very tip of your chin.

Why know your shape?

  • Haircuts: A round face needs volume at the crown, a square face needs soft curls near the face, and almost anything suits an oval face.
  • Glasses: The geometry of the glasses should contrast with the face. A round face needs square frames, a square face needs aviators or round ones.
  • Brows: The shape of the eyebrows can visually adjust the width of the forehead and “open” the gaze.
