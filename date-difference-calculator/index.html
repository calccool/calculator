Online date difference calculator

An online date difference calculator allows you to accurately calculate the amount days, weeks, months, years, hours and minutes between two dates, including or excluding the end date. It also counts weekdays and weekends, which is especially convenient for accountants, project managers, students and anyone who plans deadlines.

Why is this calculator better than analogs?

  • Counts not only days, but also hours and minutes.
  • Takes into account start and end time.
  • Allows you to include the end date in the calculation.
  • Counts separately weekdays and weekends.
  • Works in the browser without downloads and is instantly updated.

Calculation formulas

The calculator relies on simple but accurate mathematics.

1. Difference in milliseconds:

Δt = t₂ − t₁

Wheret₁— start date and time,t₂— end date and time.

2. Unit transfers:

  • Days = Δt / 86,400,000
  • Hours = Δt / 3,600,000
  • Minutes = Δt / 60,000

3. Calendar difference:

Y = y₂ − y₁
M = m₂ − m₁
D = d₂ − d₁ (с коррекцией для отрицательных значений)

Frequently Asked Questions

Can time be taken into account?
Yes, enter the hours and minutes in the additional fields - the calculation will be carried out accurate to the minute.

How to enable end date?
Select Yes in the Include End Date field and the calculator will add one day to the result.

Does the tool count holidays?
Yes, in addition to general calendar days, the calculator shows the number of working days and weekends separately.

Does it work offline?
Yes, calculations are performed directly in the browser, without connecting to the server.
