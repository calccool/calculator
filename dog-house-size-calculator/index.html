Dog house size calculator: online calculation by breed and size

Comfortable housing for a dog is not a “palace” for growth, but a functional space where the animal can warm itself with its own warmth. A booth that is too spacious will make you feel cold in the winter, and a booth that is too cramped will lead to joint diseases.

Our calculator uses gold standard of canine calculations to determine the parameters based on the actual size of your pet or its membership in a specific breed group.

How to measure a dog correctly for calculations

To get an accurate result, you will need three key measurements. Carry them out when the dog is standing in a calm state:

  1. Length(L): Distance from the tip of the nose to the base of the tail. This is the basis for determining the depth and width of the sleeping area.
  2. Height (H): From the floor to the tips of the ears (or the top of the head if the ears are drooping). This parameter determines the height of the ceiling so that the dog can stand inside without crouching.
  3. Chest Width (W): The widest part is at the front. Necessary for calculating the comfortable width of the entrance (manhole).

Sizing formulas

The calculator makes calculations based on algorithms that take into account allowances for free movement and turning of the animal indoors.

Internal parameters

In order for the dog to comfortably stretch out and curl up, the following dependencies are used:

  • Booth depth ( $D$ ): $$D = L + 15\text{ см}$$
  • Booth width ( $W_{int}$ ): $$W_{int} = L \times 0.8 + 10\text{ см}$$
  • Ceiling height ( $H_{int}$ ): $$H_{int} = H + 10\text{ см}$$

Entrance parameters

The opening should be loose enough for the dog to enter without effort, but not too large so as not to let out heat:

  • Manhole width: $$W_{ent} = W + 10\text{ см}$$
  • Manhole height: $$H_{ent} = H \times 0.8$$

Accounting for insulation and materials

An important feature of our calculator is that it takes into account wall thickness. If you are building a “winter” version using foam plastic or mineral wool, the external dimensions of the structure will increase significantly.

The calculator automatically adds the insulation thickness to the internal dimensions, giving you the final external dimensions for purchasing lumber. The system also calculates the approximate surface area:

  • Floor area: $$S_{floor} = W_{int} \times D$$
  • Roof area: The overhang (overhang) for protection from precipitation is taken into account (approximately $+30\%$ to the floor area).
  • Wall area: The sum of the areas of all four sides minus the hole for the manhole.

Why use this calculator

Millimeter precision

Unlike simple tables, we allow you to customize the calculation. You see a visual drawing of the facade and a diagram of material consumption, which helps to estimate the construction budget in advance.

Taking care of your health

Correctly calculated ceiling height and opening width prevent drafts and ensure proper air circulation. We recommend making a hole not in the center of the facade, but offset to the side - this will create a “blind” warm corner inside, protected from direct wind.
