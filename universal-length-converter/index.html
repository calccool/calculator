Mathematical translation model

All calculations in the converter are carried out through reduction to the base unit - meter. The calculation formula is as follows:

$$L_{res} = V_{in} \cdot \frac{K_{from}}{K_{to}}$$

Where:

  • $L_{res}$ - total value in target units;
  • $V_{in}$ - user entered number;
  • $K_{from}$ — conversion factor of the original unit into meters;
  • $K_{to}$ — conversion factor of the target unit into meters.
