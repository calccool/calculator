Mathematical calculation model

To ensure maximum accuracy, the calculator uses the following formulas:

  1. Tip amount ( $T$ ): $$T = B \times \frac{P}{100}$$ Where $B$ is the amount on the check, $P$ is the tip percentage.
  2. Grand total ( $G$ ): $$G = B + T$$
  3. Share per person ( $S$ ): $$S = \frac{G}{N}$$ Where $N$ is the number of people in the company.
