Online dismissal date calculator: calculation of 2-week work according to the Labor Code of the Russian Federation

Dismissal at will is a procedure clearly regulated by the Labor Code of the Russian Federation. The main difficulty for an employee is to correctly determine the last working day, since errors in dates can lead to absenteeism or delay in issuing documents.

Our calculator automates this process, taking into account the norms Article 80 of the Labor Code of the Russian Federation, and helps not only determine the timing, but also predict the final payment.

How the counting algorithm works

According to the law, the notice period for dismissal begins to be calculated not on the day of filing the application, but on next day after its receipt by the employer.

Main development dates in 2026:

  • 14 days - standard time for most employees.
  • 3 days — if you are on a probationary period or working under a short-term contract (up to 2 months).
  • 1 month — for heads of organizations and professional athletes/coaches (in some cases).
  • 0 days - upon retirement, enrollment in an educational institution or by agreement of the parties.

Mathematical calculation model

To calculate the date of dismissal, a simple but strict formula is used:

$$D_{end} = D_{sub} + T_{notice}$$

Where:

  • $D_{end}$ - last working day (date of dismissal);
  • $D_{sub}$ —date of application;
  • $T_{notice}$ — warning period (working out) in calendar days.

If you use an extended financial block, compensation for unused vacation ( $S_{comp}$ ) is calculated using the formula:

$$S_{comp} = V_{days} \cdot R_{daily}$$

Where:

  • $V_{days}$ - remaining vacation days;
  • $R_{daily}$ is your average daily earnings.

Special case: Vacation followed by dismissal

In the "Leave with subsequent" mode, the date of dismissal and the last day of work do not coincide.

  1. Day of dismissal is considered the last day of vacation. This date is entered into the work book.
  2. Last day of work (and the day of issuance of all payments) is considered to be the day preceding the first day of vacation.

This is important to know, since once the vacation begins, it is no longer possible to withdraw your resignation letter.

What is the employer required to provide on the day of dismissal?

On the last working day that our calculator calculates, you should receive:

  • Work book (or STD-R certificate);
  • Certificate of income and tax amounts of an individual;
  • Full cash payment (including salary arrears and vacation compensation);
  • An extract from personalized information about individuals.
