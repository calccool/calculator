Website development cost calculator: online price calculation by the hour

Planning a budget for creating a web resource is a process that requires taking into account many variables: from database architecture to the complexity of animations in the interface. Our calculator uses hourly assessment methodology (Time & Materials), which is the gold standard in the professional development industry.

Unlike simple calculators, this tool analyzes the project according to four key vectors: labor costs for layout, design, backend logic and integration of additional modules.

How is the total calculated?

The calculator algorithm is based on summing the base hours for each stage and multiplying them by the specialist’s rate. The mathematical calculation model is as follows:

$$T_{total} = (H_{base} + H_{design} + H_{backend} + H_{features}) \cdot K_{pages}$$

Where:

  • $T_{total}$ - total number of standard hours for the project.
  • $H_{base}$ - basic hours per structure depending on the type of site.
  • $H_{design}$ - time for UX/UI design.
  • $H_{backend}$ - setting up the server part and CMS.
  • $H_{features}$ - additional functional modules.

The final cost of the project ( $C_{total}$ ) is calculated using the formula:

$$C_{total} = T_{total} \cdot R_{hour}$$

Where $R_{hour}$ is the selected hourly rate (Rate) of the performer in rubles.

Factors influencing the final estimate

When using a questionnaire calculator, it is important to consider additional options that can significantly change the amount of work:

Visual design (Design)

  • Ready template: 0 additional hours. Selected on a tight budget.
  • Unique Design: from 20 hours. Rendering in Figma from scratch.
  • Premium UI/UX: from 45 hours. Includes complex animations, micro-interactions and 3D graphics.

Technology Stack (CMS)

  • Static HTML: Ideal for fast, high-performance pages without a control panel.
  • WordPress / OpenCart: a standard for business websites that requires customization of modules and themes.
  • Framework (Laravel/Node.js): used for custom solutions where standard CMS are powerless.

Additional modules and integrations

  1. SEO optimization: Preparation of meta tags, Schema.org micro markup and site map (from 6 hours).
  2. Acquiring: Integration of payment gateways YuKassa, Stripe or banks (from 12 hours).
  3. CRM / 1C: Synchronization of orders, product balances and customer data via API (from 20 hours).
  4. Multilingual: Implementation of a structure to support multiple language versions (from 10 hours).

Estimation of implementation time

The timing directly depends on the total hours. Our calculator calculates the time based on 32-35 productive hours per week per specialist.

  • If the project takes up to 40 hours, the implementation period will be about 1 week.
  • Projects over 150 hours are transferred to the medium-term category (from 1 month).

It is important to understand that this is “pure” operating time. In reality, you should add to this the time for communication, testing and editing, which usually amounts to an additional 15–20% of the final result.
