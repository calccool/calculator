Enterprise profit calculator GTA Online: Bunker and MS business

Launching and maintaining businesses in GTA Online is the basis of passive and active income. However, without accurate calculations it is easy to go into the red, especially when purchasing raw materials without established improvements to equipment and personnel. Our calculator allows you to instantly determine whether you should sell in the current session or wait for more favorable conditions.

Main factors influencing profit

To accurately forecast revenue, we consider four critical variables:

  • Upgrades: The presence of purchased equipment and hired personnel increases the base cost of the product and the speed of its production.
  • Selling distance: Selecting a distant region (for example, Blaine County to Los Santos) gives an automatic $x1.5$ (+ $50\%$ ) multiplier to the final price.
  • High Demand Bonus: For each player in the session who is not your ally, Rockstar Games awards a bonus.
  • Event weeks: Periodic promotions increase payouts by $1.5$ or $2$ times.

Mathematical calculation model

The following formula is used to calculate the total amount that will be credited to your Maze Bank account:

$$S = (V_{base} \times M_{event} \times M_{dist}) \times (1 + (P_{players} \times 0.025))$$

Where:

  • $S$ - total sale amount.
  • $V_{base}$ - basic cost of the product (depending on the type of business and upgrades).
  • $M_{event}$ - bonus week multiplier ( $1$ , $1.5$ or $2$ ).
  • $M_{dist}$ - distance multiplier ( $1.5$ for long-distance delivery).
  • $P_{players}$ — number of competitors in the session (max. $20$ ).

Net profit ( $P_{net}$ ) is calculated by deducting the costs of purchasing raw materials ( $C_{supplies}$ ):

$$P_{net} = S — C_{supplies}$$

Strategy "Maximum income"

To make your business investments pay off faster, adhere to the following rules:

  1. Never buy raw materials without improvements. In the document forgery business, purchasing raw materials without upgrades will result in a net loss.
  2. Use the session bonus. Selling in a populated session (20+ people) adds $50\%$ to the amount. This covers the risk of attack if you use the organization's abilities (for example, “Hide organization” for Ghost Organization).
  3. Sell ​​one batch. If you play solo, sell the goods after one purchase of raw materials ( $75 000$ ). This ensures that only one delivery vehicle is available.

Instructions for the calculator

To get an accurate result, follow three steps:

  1. Select mode: "Filled Warehouse" to estimate full capacity or "Single Lot" for a quick sale.
  2. Please indicate your business type and current bonus of the week.
  3. Enter the number of players in the session - the calculator will automatically add $2.5\%$ for each “rival”.
