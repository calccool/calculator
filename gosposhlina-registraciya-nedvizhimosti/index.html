State duty calculator for registering property rights

Registration of property rights in Rosreestr is the final and most important stage of any real estate transaction. The amount of the state fee (state duty) is strictly regulated by the Tax Code of the Russian Federation. Our calculator is designed taking into account all the nuances of the law, including the automatic distribution of payments between co-owners upon shared registration.

Calculation algorithm and formulas

If an object is purchased by several persons at the same time, the total amount of the fee is divided by the number of future owners. This rule applies if the purchase and sale or gift agreement does not indicate a different distribution of shares.

The basic formula for calculating an individual payment is as follows:

$$P_{user} = \frac{P_{base}}{n}$$

Where:

  • $P_{user}$ - total amount payable for one applicant;
  • $P_{base}$ - basic duty rate for a specific type of object and category of person;
  • $n$ - the total number of owners participating in the registration.

If the applicant is entitled to a tax benefit according to Art. 333.35 of the Tax Code of the Russian Federation, then the following condition applies:

$$P_{user} = 0$$

Categories of citizens exempt from payment

The legislation of the Russian Federation provides for a number of exceptions when registration of property rights is free of charge. The interface of our calculator provides a switch “There are benefits”, which resets the payment amount for the following categories:

  • Low-income citizens (with the appropriate certificate);
  • Veterans and disabled people of the Great Patriotic War;
  • Prisoners of concentration camps and ghettos during the Second World War;
  • Individuals when registering rights to objects damaged as a result of emergency situations.
