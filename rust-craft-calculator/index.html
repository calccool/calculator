Crafting mathematics and basic formulas

The logic of the calculator is based on standard recipes of Vanilla servers. To understand the scale of production, we use the following mathematical relationships:

  1. Gunpowder calculation: $$G = S \cdot 2 + C \cdot 3$$ where $G$ is a unit of gunpowder, $S$ is sulfur, $C$ is coal.
  2. Explosives cost: $$E = G \cdot 50 + L \cdot 3 + S \cdot 10 + M \cdot 10$$ where $E$ is explosive, $L$ is low quality fuel (TNK), $M$ is metal fragments.
  3. Total sulfur equivalent for C4: To create one C4 charge, $2200$ units of pure sulfur are required (including all intermediate crafting).

How to use the calculator

To get the most accurate result, follow this algorithm:

  1. Select category: The Explosives tab for planning raids or Weapons and Armor for preparing for PVP.
  2. Specify quantity: Use the input field or speed dial buttons.
  3. Select detail mode: * Standard: will show how much gunpowder and explosives you need to craft.
    • Raw material mode: will show how much iron and sulfur ore needs to be loaded into the furnaces.
  4. Analyze the diagram: Estimate the total mass of resources at the bottom of the visualization block.

Why is this important for a raider?

Planning your resources allows you to avoid running out of materials mid-craft. For example, knowing that for 10 missiles exactly required 14,000 sulfur And 19,500 coal, you can distribute the loot among group members in advance. Our calculator even takes into account such little things as the cost of fabric for wicks and TNCs for the production of explosives, which makes it the most accurate tool in the CIS segment.
