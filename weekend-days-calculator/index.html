Mathematical calculation model

To ensure high accuracy, the calculator operates with formulas that take into account astronomical and calendar features.

Determining the year type

The first step is to determine the number of days in a year ( $D_{total}$ ) by checking for the presence of February 29th:

$$D_{total} = 365 + L$$

Where $L = 1$ if the year is a leap year (divisible by 4 without a remainder, except for century years not divisible by 400), and $0$ in other cases.

Calculation of the final balance

The total number of working days ( $D_{work}$ ) is calculated using the formula:

$$D_{work} = D_{total} — (D_{weekend} + D_{holiday} + D_{vacation})$$

  • $D_{weekend}$ — number of regular days off according to the selected schedule.
  • $D_{holiday}$ - official holidays.
  • $D_{vacation}$ - vacation days falling during working hours.
