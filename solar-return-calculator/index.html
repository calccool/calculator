Solar (or Solar Return) is one of the most effective methods of predictive astrology. It records the moment when the transiting Sun returns to the same degree, minute and second of the zodiac in which it was at the moment of your birth.

This moment is your true astrological birthday. It is at this second that the “program code” of events for the coming year is laid. Our calculator not only calculates the date, but also analyzes Energy mandala, determining which area of ​​life your main resource will be directed to.

Astronomical calculation principle

Solar's calculation is based on the average length of the tropical year. Since the calendar year (365 days) does not coincide with the actual period of the Earth’s revolution around the Sun, the time of the return of the luminary shifts by about 6 hours every year.

To calculate the exact moment, use the formula for finding the position of the Sun $L_{\odot}$ :

$$L_{\odot} = \lambda_{birth} \pmod{360^{\circ}}$$

Where $\lambda_{birth}$ is the ecliptic longitude of the Sun at the moment of birth. The task of the calculator is to find the moment in time $T$ at which:

$$L_{\odot}(T) — L_{\odot}(T_{birth}) = 0$$
