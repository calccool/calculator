HBO payback calculator: Calculation of real benefits and break-even point

Installation of gas cylinder equipment (LPG) is an investment project within one vehicle. Like any investment, it has CAPEX (capital costs for installation) and OPEX (operating expenses).

Most simple calculators on the Internet make a critical mistake: they calculate the difference in fuel prices directly. Our algorithm uses the formula "Honest Savings", taking into account factors that installers are often silent about:

  1. Thermal efficiency: Gas has a lower energy density than gasoline, which leads to an inevitable increase in consumption by 10-20%.
  2. Cold start: Any modern gas equipment (4th generation and higher) uses gasoline to warm the gearbox to operating temperature (usually +40°C).
  3. Depreciation: The need to replace gas filters (liquid and vapor phase) every 10,000 km.

Formula for calculating break-even point

To determine the exact mileage after which you will start making a net profit, we use the following equation:

$$L_{payback} = \frac{C_{install}}{ \left( \frac{R_{gas} \times P_{gas}}{100} + C_{maint} + C_{warm} \right) — \left( \frac{R_{petrol} \times P_{petrol}}{100} \right) }$$

Where:

  • $L_{payback}$ — Payback mileage (km).
  • $C_{install}$ — Equipment installation cost.
  • $R_{gas}$ - Gas consumption (usually $R_{petrol} \times 1.15$ ).
  • $P_{gas}$ / $P_{petrol}$ - Price per liter of gas and gasoline.
  • $C_{maint}$ — Cost of servicing gas equipment per 1 km.
  • $C_{warm}$ — The cost of gasoline for warming up per 1 km.

Hidden nuances of using gas equipment

To ensure that the calculator shows the most accurate data, pay attention to the following parameters:

  • Excessive gas consumption: For propane-butane, it is considered normal to increase consumption by 15-20% compared to gasoline. If the consumption is above 25%, the system requires adjustment of the fuel map. For methane the coefficient is about 0.8-0.9 (in cubic meters), but the equipment is more expensive.
  • Mileage per year: This is the main animator. If you drive less than 10,000 km per year, the payback period may take 2-3 years, which makes installation impractical given inflation and the loss of value of the car.
  • Registration with the traffic police: Don’t forget to include in the “Installation cost” field state fees and services for legalizing design changes (from 5 to 15 thousand rubles) if you plan to formalize the equipment.

Types of equipment and their impact on ROI

  1. 2nd generation: Cheap, installed on carburetor cars. It pays for itself instantly, but increases consumption by 20-30%.
  2. 4th generation (Distributed injection): Gold standard. Optimal balance of price, consumption (10-15%) and dynamics.
  3. 5th and 6th generation (Liquid injection): It starts immediately on gas (no expenses for gasoline), but the installation cost is 2-3 times higher, which pushes back the payback point.
