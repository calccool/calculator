Percentage Standard Deviation Calculator: Sample and Population Calculation

In statistical data analysis, there is often a need to estimate volatility or the spread of values ​​expressed as a percentage. Our standard deviation percentage calculator is a professional tool that allows you to instantly process an array of data, calculate the degree of their heterogeneity and prepare the basis for in-depth analysis.

Why is it important to calculate variance for percentages?

Percentages are often used in finance (stock returns), marketing (conversion rates), and sociology (survey results). A simple arithmetic average can be deceptive: for example, two sets of data with an average of 10% may have completely different risks if in one case the values ​​are clustered around 10% and in the other they are scattered from 0% to 20%.

Basic calculation formulas

The calculator is based on classical formulas of mathematical statistics.

1. Arithmetic mean ( $\bar{x}$ ):

$$\bar{x} = \frac{\sum_{i=1}^{n} x_i}{n}$$

2. Dispersion ( $s^2$ or $\sigma^2$ ):

  • For Population: $$\sigma^2 = \frac{\sum (x_i — \bar{x})^2}{n}$$
  • For Sample: $$s^2 = \frac{\sum (x_i — \bar{x})^2}{n-1}$$

3. Standard Deviation ( $\sigma$ ):

$$\sigma = \sqrt{\text{Дисперсия}}$$

How to use the calculator
