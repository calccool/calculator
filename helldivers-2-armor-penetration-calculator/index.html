Helldivers 2 Armor Penetration Calculator: Weapons and Stratagems

To effectively spread managed democracy, it is not enough to simply pull the trigger. Helldivers 2 has hidden mechanics Armor Penetration (AP) determines whether your shot will do full damage, half damage, or ricochet uselessly. Our calculator allows you to instantly compare the characteristics of your equipment with the target's protection class.

The penetration to armor ratio always results in one of three scenarios:

  • Full Damage (Green Hit/Red Marker): Your AP is higher than the target's armor level. The projectile passes through the defense, dealing 100% damage.
  • Reduced damage (White Marker): Your AP is exactly equal to your armor level. The projectile penetrates the shell, but loses energy, causing only 50% damage.
  • Ricochet (Shield Icon): Your AP is below armor level. The projectile bounces back, dealing 0% damage.

Formula for calculating effective penetration

In addition to the basic characteristics of the weapon, the angle of entry of the projectile affects the result. The game has the concept of "Glancing hit" ( Glancing Hit ), which dynamically reduces penetration power.

Mathematically it looks like this:

$$AP_{eff} = AP_{base} — P_{angle}$$

Where:

  • $AP_{eff}$ - final value of armor penetration;
  • $AP_{base}$ - basic weapon indicator;
  • $P_{angle}$ - angle penalty (equal to 1, if the hit is not perpendicular to the plane of the armor).

The final percentage of damage dealt ( $D$ ) is calculated according to the following condition:

$$D = \begin{cases} 100\%, & \text{если } AP_{eff} > Armor \\ 50\%, & \text{если } AP_{eff} = Armor \\ 0\%, & \text{если } AP_{eff} < Armor \end{cases}$$

Classification of enemy armor and weapons

To use the calculator effectively, you need to understand the categories of the main threats on the battlefield. Below is a summary table of the most current Armor Level values.

Enemy Armor Levels Table

Categories of armor penetration of stratagems

  1. Light (AP 2-3): Effective against infantry and weak points (Machine Guns, Laser Pistol).
  2. Average (AP 4): The gold standard for fighting Ravagers and Hive Guardians (Autocannon, Lasercannon).

  4. Ultimate (AP 7+): Orbital strikes that ignore almost any defense.

Why Glance Hits is Important

Many players wonder why their Autocannon (AP 4) sometimes ricochets off the Ravager's shield (Armor 4). The answer lies in the angle of attack. If you shoot at a sharp angle, your effective penetration drops to 3, which is below the shield's armor level.

Tips for breaking through:

  • Try to shoot as perpendicular to the surface of the armor as possible.
  • Use the calculator to see if the Railgun will help you in "unsafe" mode against a specific enemy body part.
  • Remember that explosive damage (AOE) often has its own penetration parameters that are different from a direct projectile hit.
