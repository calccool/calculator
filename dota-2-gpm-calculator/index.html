GPM and Net Worth calculator in Dota 2: Online calculation using cryptostat

In Dota 2 indicator GPM (Gold Per Minute) is a key marker of player effectiveness, especially in the carry and midlaner positions. Our calculator allows you to instantly convert the number of creeps killed (CS) and participation in battles into specific figures for the total value (Net Worth) and gold rate.

Calculating GPM is important to understand whether you have time to buy key artifacts (for example, Battle Fury or Radiance) in time for the timings accepted in the professional meta.

Mathematical model for calculating gold

The calculator algorithm is based on weighted average values ​​of the current patch. Your total value ( $NW$ ) comes from several sources:

  1. Starting capital: 600 gold.
  2. Passive income: 100 gold per minute (awarded after the creeps are released).
  3. Crypstat: Average reward for a lane or jungle creep.
  4. Combat activity: Gold for kills, assistance and destruction of buildings.

GPM formula

To calculate the final indicator, the following formula is used:

$$Total Gold = 600 + (T \cdot 100) + (CS_{lane} \cdot 40) + (CS_{jungle} \cdot 40) + (K \cdot 250) + (A \cdot 50) + (Tr \cdot 100)$$

Where:

  • $T$ — match time in minutes;
  • $CS$ - number of last-killed creeps (Lane/Jungle);
  • $K$ - Kills;
  • $A$ - help (Assists);
  • $Tr$ - destroyed towers.

Final forecast:

$$GPM = \frac{Total Gold}{T}$$

Average value of game objects

For an accurate forecast, we use average values, since the reward for creeps and heroes in Dota 2 is dynamic and depends on the time and level of value of the victim.

Analysis of farming efficiency by role

After the calculation, the calculator assigns you a “Grade” - an estimate of your pace. This helps you understand if you are completing your task on the map.

Basic pace indicators (Benchmarks)

  • Low GPM (less than 400): Typical for position 4 and 5 supports. Your job is to provide visibility and save cows, not farm.
  • Average GPM (400–550): The norm for offlaners and active midlaners who spend a lot of time moving and ganking.
  • Good GPM (550–750): An indicator of quality carry. You effectively combine farming lines and jungles without wasting time.
  • Esports GPM (above 750): Hard Carry level. This pace is usually achieved with perfect laning and the use of abilities to speed up farming (for example, Anti-Mage's Blink or Alchemist's blades).

How to raise GPM: Tips from pro players

If your forecast shows low values, pay attention to the following aspects:

  • Packet control: Never miss a “pack” of creeps in the lane for the sake of a dubious frag at the beginning of the game.
  • Stacks: Ask supports to stack in the jungle - this increases your income by 20-30% in the midgame.
  • Timings: Try to take catapults at 5, 10 and 15 minutes, as they give more gold and allow you to push towers faster.
  • Finishing under the tower: Train last hit so as not to lose gold when the enemy pins you under the T1 tower.
