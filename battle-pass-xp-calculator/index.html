Battle pass calculator: how many days to play until max. level?

The Battle Pass is the primary way to earn cosmetic rewards in modern games. However, the main difficulty lies in timing: will you be able to collect the “secret skin” or reach the maximum level before the season ends?

Our interactive calculator allows you to instantly evaluate your progress in two modes: Time Estimation (how many days left to play) and Tempo estimation (what is the average experience per day required).

Mathematical calculation model

Most games (such as Fortnite, Valorant, Apex Legends) are based on a linear progression system. This means that the amount of experience for moving from 5th to 6th level is identical to moving from 95th to 96th.

To calculate the remaining experience, use the following formula:

$$XP_{total} = ((L_{target} — L_{current}) \cdot XP_{level}) — XP_{progress}$$

Where:

  • $L_{target}$ - Desired level is usually 100 or 200.
  • $L_{current}$ - Your current level in the game.
  • $XP_{level}$ - The amount of experience required for one promotion.
  • $XP_{progress}$ - Already gained experience at the current level.

To calculate time (number of days), the formula is used:

$$Days = \lceil XP_{total} / (XP_{daily} + (XP_{weekly} / 7)) \rceil$$

How to use the calculator

To get the most accurate forecast, follow these instructions:

  1. Select mode: “Time Estimate” will show the completion date of farming, and “Pace Estimate” will tell you how much experience per day you need to grind if there is little time left until the end of the season.
  2. Enter levels: Indicate what stage you are at now and what goal you are pursuing.
  3. Set up XP: Use presets (Fortnite, Valorant, Apex) or enter your own value.
  4. Consider the current progress: Turn on the switch “Take into account current progress at the level” if you want precise accuracy up to a unit of experience.
  5. Add quests: Take into account not only pure grind, but also the average experience from weekly tasks.

Tips for speeding up your leveling

If the calculator shows that you are behind schedule, use the following optimization methods:

  • Focus on Weekly Challenges: They always give 5-10 times more experience than regular matches.
  • Modes with accelerated XP: In Fortnite this could be “Creative” or “Lego”, in shooters it could be modes like “Deathmatch”.
  • Playing in a group: Many Battle Passes give a +10-20% experience bonus for playing with friends.
  • Double XP Events: Keep an eye on the developer calendar; such events usually take place in the last two weeks of the season.
