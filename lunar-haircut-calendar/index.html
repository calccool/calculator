Golden rules for using a calendar

  • For fast growth: Plan a visit to the salon during the first or second quarter of the waxing Moon.
  • To strengthen the roots: If your hair falls out, choose the decreasing phase - growth will slow down, but the hair follicles will become much stronger.
  • Coloring: The pigment lasts longer and lies more evenly on the growing Moon. As the color fades, it washes off faster, which is useful if you are experimenting with toner.
  • Perm: Avoid Leo days (the curls will be too fine) and choose Virgo days for perfect waves.
