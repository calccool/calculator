How does the numerological calculation of the Arcana of the day work?

The technique is based on a synthesis of classical numerology and the wisdom of the Tarot. It is based on calculating the sum of the numbers of two key dates: the moment of your birth and the day for which the forecast is made.

Calculation formula

The mathematical model for calculating an individual Arcana is as follows:

$$A = ( \sum d_{birth} + \sum d_{target} ) \pmod{22}$$

Where:

  • $A$ is the final number of the Major Arcana.
  • $\sum d_{birth}$ - the sum of all digits of the date of birth (day, month, year).
  • $\sum d_{target}$ is the sum of all digits of the date of interest.
  • $\pmod{22}$ - reduction to a cycle of 22 Arcana (if the sum is greater than 22, 22 is subtracted from it to obtain a number in the range from 1 to 22).

Important clarification: In this system, the 22nd Arcana (Jester) is often equated to zero or the number 22, depending on the school; our calculator uses the classic cycle, where 22 is the final and at the same time the starting point of the path.
