Calculator for calculating materials for an arched greenhouse

Construction of an arched greenhouse is one of the most economical and effective ways of arranging a vegetable garden. Thanks to their streamlined shape, such structures are highly resistant to wind loads, and the absence of sharp corners significantly extends the service life of the covering material. However, it is almost impossible to accurately calculate materials “by eye” due to the complex geometry of the arc. Our professional online calculator allows you to instantly obtain accurate data for the purchase of polycarbonate and metal profiles, eliminating unnecessary costs and shortages of materials during the installation process.

Main features of the calculator

  • Arch geometry calculation: determining the exact arc length and segment height.
  • Cutting polycarbonate: the calculator converts the total glazing area into the number of standard sheets (2.1x6 m or 2.1x12 m).
  • Frame footage: calculation of the total length of the profile pipe for arches, longitudinal girders and base.
  • End calculation: calculation of the area of ​​facades (doors and windows) for precise closing of the ends.
  • Consumables: determining the required number of thermal washers and self-tapping screws for reliable fastening.

For correct design, it is important to understand that the surface area of ​​the arched part depends on the length of the arc, which is calculated using mathematical formulas for a circle segment.

Mathematical basis of calculation

  1. Arc Length: If the greenhouse is a perfect semicircle, the formula is simple: $L_{arc} = \pi \times R$ (where $R$ is the radius or half the width). In cases where the height ( $H$ ) is not equal to the radius, the segment chord and height formula is used: $L_{arc} = 2 \times R \times \arcsin(\frac{W}{2 \times R})$
  2. Greenhouse volume (Volume): Important for calculating the power of heating or ventilation systems: $V = S_{face} \times L$ (where $S_{face}$ is the end area, $L$ is the length of the greenhouse).
