How does the rising sign calculation work?

The Earth makes a full revolution around its axis in 24 hours, so the Ascendant point passes through all 12 signs of the Zodiac within one day. On average, the sign changes every 2 hours, which explains why people born on the same day can have completely different characters and destinies.

For accurate calculations, geographic coordinates (latitude and longitude) and exact local time are used, which are then converted to sidereal time.

Mathematical calculation model

The algorithm is based on determining the position of the cusp of the 1st house. For this, the formula for the projection of the ecliptic onto the horizon is used. The simplified logic for determining sidereal time ( $LST$ ) is as follows:

$$LST = 6.6460656 + 2400.0513 \cdot T + 1.0027379 \cdot UT$$

Where:

  • $T$ - the number of centuries that have passed since the J2000.0 epoch.
  • $UT$ - Coordinated Universal Time.

After finding $LST$ and taking into account the latitude of the area ( $\phi$ ), the longitude of the Ascendant ( $\lambda$ ) is calculated through the arctangent:

$$\lambda = \arctan\left(\frac{-\cos(LST)}{\sin(LST) \cdot \cos(\epsilon) + \tan(\phi) \cdot \sin(\epsilon)}\right)$$

Where $\epsilon$ is the inclination of the ecliptic (approximately 23.4° ).

Why is it important to know the exact time of birth?

Since point Asc moves at a speed of approximately 1 degree in 4 minutes, even a small time error can lead to an error in the calculations.

  1. Change of sign: If you were born on a sign border, a 5-minute difference can turn a "quiet" Cancer Ascendant into a "royal" Leo.
  2. Grid of houses: All other 11 astrological houses, which are responsible for finances, marriage, career and health, are counted from the Ascendant.
  3. Horoscope ruler: The planet ruling your rising sign becomes the major planet in your chart (Herald), indicating the main area of ​​fulfillment in life.

FAQ: Frequently asked questions

Is it possible to find out the Ascendant without the time of birth?

Unfortunately, without time the calculation will be inaccurate. However, you can use the method rectification - this is a reverse calculation of time for important events in life (wedding, birth of children, moving).

What should I do if my Ascendant is in an “intercepted” sign?

This occurs in northern latitudes, where some signs rise very quickly or very slowly. Our calculator takes into account these astronomical nuances for the most correct result.

How does the Ascendant affect appearance?

An Aries Ascendant often gives an athletic build and sharp facial features, while a Taurus Ascendant tends to favor soft lines and great physical stamina. Libra rising people often have an innate sense of style and symmetrical facial features.
