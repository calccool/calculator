STS calculator 2026: tax calculation 6% and 15% online for individual entrepreneurs and LLCs

The simplified taxation system (STS) remains the most popular regime for small and medium-sized businesses. Our tool allows you to instantly calculate the amount of tax payable, taking into account all the nuances of the law, including tax deductions and “minimum tax” rules.

STS “Income” (Rate 6%)

In this model, the entire amount of revenue received to the current account or cash register is taxed. The main advantage is the ability to reduce the calculated tax by the amount of insurance premiums paid.

Tax calculation algorithm:

$$Налог = Доход \times Ставка — Вычет$$

Where the “Deduction” is formed according to the following rules:

  • Individual entrepreneur without employees: can reduce tax on 100% insurance premiums paid for yourself.
  • Individual entrepreneur with employees and LLC: can reduce tax by no more than 50% from the calculated amount.

STS “Income minus expenses” (Rate 15%)

This option is beneficial for businesses with a high share of confirmed costs (production, trade). Tax is paid on the difference between income and expenses.

Basic formula:

$$Налоговая\,база = Доходы — Расходы — Взносы$$

$$Налог = Налоговая\,база \times Ставка$$

Important rule: Minimum tax

On the simplified tax system of 15% there is the concept of a minimum tax. If at the end of the year the amount of calculated tax (15% of profit) turns out to be less than 1% of total income, the entrepreneur is obliged to pay exactly this 1%.

$$MinTax = Доход \times 0.01$$

How to use the calculator

To get an accurate result, follow these instructions:

  1. Select mode: switch the tab to the desired rate (6% or 15%).
  2. Enter your income: indicate the total amount of receipts for the billing period.
  3. Please indicate your expenses: (for simplified tax system 15%) enter the amount of confirmed costs.
  4. Additional options: turn on the “Take into account contributions” toggle switch if you have already made payments this year. This will allow the calculator to apply the tax deduction.
  5. Regional rate: If there are incentives in your region, change the standard value (6 or 15) to what is relevant for you.

Our calculator automatically visualizes the structure of your finances, showing the share of net profit, taxes and expenses in the total turnover, which allows you to clearly assess the tax burden.
