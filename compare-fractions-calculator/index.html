Ways to Compare Fractions

There are two main mathematical methods that our algorithm uses to prove the result.

Method 1: Reduction to a common denominator (CCD)

To compare the fractions $\frac{A}{B}$ and $\frac{C}{D}$ , you need to make sure that their lower parts (denominators) become the same.

  1. Find the Least Common Multiple (LCM) for $B$ and $D$ .
  2. Multiply the numerator and denominator of the first fraction by an additional factor.
  3. We repeat the same for the second fraction.
  4. Let's compare the new numerators.

Comparison formula:

$$\frac{A \times D}{B \times D} \quad \text{vs} \quad \frac{C \times B}{D \times B}$$

Method 2: Decimal Conversion

This is the fastest way for household assessment. We simply divide the numerator by the denominator.

  • $\frac{1}{2} = 0.5$
  • $\frac{1}{3} \approx 0.333$
  • Since $0.5 > 0.333$ , so is $\frac{1}{2} > \frac{1}{3}$ .
