Main calculation indicators

The calculator analyzes 5 key parameters of your personality. Understanding these numbers provides the key to unlocking your potential and adjusting your life scenario.

1. The Life Path Number

This is the most important indicator in the numerology chart. It is immutable and is determined by your date of birth. This is your “corridor of opportunity” - the talents with which you came into this world, and the lessons that you have to learn.

Calculation formula:

$$LifePath = \sum(Day) + \sum(Month) + \sum(Year)$$

Where $\sum$ is the numerology abbreviation to a single digit number (or master number).

2. Expression Number

Calculated based on the full name given at birth. It shows your natural abilities, goals and what you can achieve in life. This is the vector of your development.

3. Soul Urge Number

Determined by the sum of the vowels of your name. This is your “true self”, hidden desires, motivation and what really makes you happy, even if you hide it from others.

4. Personality Number

Calculated based on the consonants of the name. This is your “social mask” - how other people perceive you when you first meet, and what qualities you project to the outside world.

5. Personal Year

A cyclical indicator that changes every birthday. It tells you what you need to focus on this year: career, family, education or relaxation.
