LTL shipping cost calculator

Delivery of groupage cargo ( Less than Truckload ) is a complex logistics process where the final price depends not only on the distance traveled, but also on how much “usable space” your product occupies in the truck. Our calculator automates complex calculations, allowing you to instantly get a transparent estimate.

Why is volumetric weight important?

In logistics there is the concept of “calculated weight”. If you're hauling heavy metal parts, you pay by physical weight. If you are sending light but bulky goods (for example, soft toys or plastic containers), the transport company (TC) will calculate based on volume.

1 m³ = 250 kg

Formulas used in calculations

To help you understand the structure of your expenses, the calculator operates with the following mathematical dependencies:

  1. Volume calculation (V): $$V = \frac{L \cdot W \cdot H}{1\,000\,000}$$ where $L$ is length, $W$ is width, $H$ is height in centimeters.
  2. Definition of design weight ( $W_{res}$ ): $$W_{res} = \max(W_{phys}, V \cdot 250)$$
  3. Basic tariff: $$Cost_{base} = 500 + (W_{res} \cdot \frac{D}{100} \cdot 2.5)$$ where $D$ is the distance in kilometers.

Additional services and extra charges

In addition to the basic terminal-to-terminal transportation, the total amount may include service options that ensure safety and convenience:

  • Cargo insurance: It is 0.5% of the declared value. This is the minimum payment for financial peace of mind when transporting expensive goods.
  • Forwarding (Pickup/Delivery): Fixed fee 800 $ + weight surcharge.
  • Special packaging: Palletizing protects against spillage, and rigid wooden sheathing is necessary to prevent mechanical damage.
  • Fragile cargo: If the product requires special handling, a coefficient of 1.2 is applied (+20% to the base tariff).

How to use a calculator: step-by-step instructions

  1. Select input mode: If you know the exact dimensions (LxWxH), use the first tab. If the volume has already been calculated, switch to “Specify ready volume”.
  2. Specify route: Enter the distance in kilometers. For your convenience, quick entry buttons have been added for popular destinations (Msk-St. Petersburg, Msk-Krd).
  3. Enter weight and dimensions: Based on this data, the system will determine which weight is dominant - physical or volumetric.
  4. Set options: Mark the need for pickup from the door or delivery to the recipient.
  5. Get the result: Click "Calculate" to see a detailed breakdown of expenses in the form of a receipt and a visual diagram of the cost structure.
