Formula for calculating final DPS

The calculator makes calculations using the following formula:

$$Total DPS = \text{BaseAvg} \times (1 + \frac{\sum \text{Inc}}{100}) \times \prod_{i=1}^{n} (1 + \frac{\text{More}_i}{100}) \times \text{APS} \times \text{CritFactor}$$

Where:

  • BaseAvg: The average value between the minimum and maximum damage from the tooltip.
  • $\sum \text{Inc}$: The sum of all your "Increased Damage" bonuses.
  • $\prod \text{More}$: The product of all multipliers from support stones.
  • APS: Speed ​​of attacks or spells cast per second.
  • CritFactor: Effective critical strike multiplier calculated as: $$1 + (\text{Chance} \times \frac{\text{Multiplier} — 100}{100})$$

How to use a calculator: step-by-step instructions

To get the most accurate result, follow this algorithm:

  1. Base Damage: Look in the game at the tooltip of your main skill (without taking into account support stones, if you want to compare the “pure” increase). Enter the minimum and maximum values.
  2. Increased %: Add up all your tree and equipment bonuses that are related to the damage type (for example, "Increase Physical Damage" or "Increase Spell Damage").
  3. ModifiersMore: Enter the percentage values ​​that are shown on your support gems (for example, Melee Physical Damage gives 49% More).
  4. Critical Strike: If your build is based on crits, turn on the corresponding switch and indicate your crit chance and multiplier from the character characteristics window.
  5. Visualization: Our tool will automatically build a diagram of your 6-link and show on the graph what share of damage each factor contributes.

Why is this important for build optimization?

Many players make the mistake of continuing to “Increased” damage on a tree when its value already exceeds 500-600%. At this point, each subsequent node of the tree gives less and less actual growth.

The main advantages of using links:

  • Exponential growth: Each new More-multiplier in a 6-link multiplies the previous result.
  • Elemental Conversion: Choosing the right damage type allows you to effectively bypass enemy resistance.
  • Stripping speed: Understanding the real DPS per hit allows you to balance attack speed and damage for a comfortable “one-shot” of packs of mobs.
